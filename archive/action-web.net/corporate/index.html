Akimbo Team Productions, LLC is the final culmination of a saga that began in the early days of Quake2 (1997), when a few friends decided to emulate the Hong Kong action masterpiece 'Hard Boiled' in-game. The working title was 'Action Quake2' - it stuck, and became one of the most popular mods ever.

The sequel, Action Half-Life, was similarly popular and built on the strengths and style of AQ2. In late 1999, the A-Team, as the makers of AQ2 had become known, were asked to work on Action Unreal Tournament, which since has evolved into something even bigger and better...

To proceed further in game development, incorporation was a logical next step - hence Akimbo Team Productions, LLC.
