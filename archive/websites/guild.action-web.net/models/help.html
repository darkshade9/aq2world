Feb.17.1999.01:05pm EST - Hammer - "Model Help"

   So you've got the latest model pack, now what? Well, its an auto-extracting archive, so just point it at your Quake2\action directory (usually C:\Quake2\action) and you're on your way.
   But wait!!! I hear you cry. I only downloaded one model, now what? Well, actually you do the exact same thing, and it should just work. If it doesn't, try to do a search on the model name and see where it did put itself, and e-mail us with a description of the problem. Thanks, and enjoy!
