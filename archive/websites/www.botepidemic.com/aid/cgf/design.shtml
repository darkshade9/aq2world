CGF (AI) Design and Features
Last update: December 16, 2000, by William.

CGF takes the Action Quake 2 mod and turns it into an immersive single player team combat experience, offering one of the most challenging tactical AIs, and (by means of the scripts) a lot of customization.

These pages provide you with more info on what CGF offers, how it was constructed, and how you can use it.:

  • overall design of the CGF AI
  • AI vision, tracking and aiming
  • tactical terrain AI
  • weapon reasoning explained [Nov 2000]
  • AI grenade handling [Dec 2000]
  • terrain (route) files for an AQ2 map
  • writing and editing CGF mission scripts
  • fog in CGF (and customizing fog for a specific map)
  • breakable glass in CGF / AQ2 1.52 (and how to add it to your map)
  • (co-op) multiplayer CGF, servers and missions
I hope this info assists you in enjoying and understanding CGF, or developing (better) bot AI yourself. If you have comments or questions (that aren't covered by the FAQ), I'd like to hear from you.
