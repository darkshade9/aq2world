Submit a map to MapZone

Your map should meet the minimum requirements for AQMD with the exception of #4. The advantage of being approved by AQMD is your map becomes official and may be included in future idsofware releases.

Note: All file names must be in lower case not only because its a requirement but the maps will be played on a Linux server.

1. Send the map as an attachment to Merlin or Smoke.
2. Include your name, email and icq if possible.
3. Send screenshots if you have then.
4. List any known problems or possible quirks.

 
Submission Guidelines

First off, No Beta test maps will be accepted unless they come from, or are brought to our attention by the mapper. Now, I would also suggest that all maps are also sent to the AQMD. The AQMD plays an important part in the Action community, they test maps, we don't. Please remember that the testers at AQMD are all experienced players and quite a few are mappers. All they can do is help you.

If you have no intention of submitting the map to AQMD please explain the reasons to me, as I keep in contact with the AQMD staff it would be helpful for me to know.

I am not worried about "illegal" textures, but the use of them will keep you from getting the map tested at AQMD and will also result in your map not being included in any future AQ2-related CD-ROMs that may be provided to the public by the AQ2 team."

If the map is to be submitted to both the AQMD and the MapZone, I would ask that you follow the submission guidelines setup by the AQMD. In addittion to these guidelines I would ask that you also include something in the name that signifies that the map is a beta. A good example of this is Vertigo, which was first submitted as Vertigo_1h. The name of the zip should be changed as well as the name of the .bsp.

If you come across a map that looks good, but wasn't designed specifically for Action let me know. Send me an e-mail and include a brief description and a place I can download the map, also include the authors name if possible.

Screen Shot Submissions: I am still looking for screen shots. Here's what I want:
1. all shots should be on empty maps.
2. all shots should be renamed after the map.
3. I reserve the right to crop and edit the shots as I see fit, mostly this is just for adjusting the file size.
