MapZone Maps - Reaction Quake 3

With Action Half-Life and Action Unreal Tournament still being created, the Action Quake 2 community were slowly getting tired of Quake 2. Then the news regarding AHL and AUT not being direct ports of AQ2, there were many dissapointed people. That's were the the AQ2 community stepped in, and started planning Reaction Quake 3. The planning became more and more exact, and with the numerous amount of people willing to help create a direct port of AQ2 to Quake 3, a team was born.

Reaction is under heavy construction, and this page is to give the anxious AQ2 players a taste of the progress of the the maps that are being ported to Q3. There are still many other maps in production, but no screen shots have been taken/the author hasn't release a public beta of it. Enjoy!
