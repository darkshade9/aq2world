MapZone server


The purpose of the server is to give mappers a chance to have their maps played and receive comments, tips and suggestions in an unoffiicial environment to hopefully get them one step closer to getting their maps approved by AQMD. MapZone runs its own port on the ActionZone server strictly for the purpose of evaluating maps. The address of the server is 24.14.245.124:27911. Everyone is encouraged to play the server and comment on the maps. To get your map into the map rotation please read the Submission rules and "submit a map"

Good luck and good mapping!!!
