Offline Counterstrike Testing !!! | @ 18:17 | shingy

Me being a nice guy and great mapper that I am, I have decided to help the Counterstrike community out.

All the time I keep hearing people ask if there is anyway to test weapons without having to join a game online and get a few frags. I'm suprised no one thought of this, but here it is, the solution to the problem. All I did was make a small room with terror and a ct spawnpoints, and then added 8 hostages. All you have to do to collect money is switch to CT, bind a key to "+use; hostage", and then hit that key on all of the hostages. Then proceed to buy your weapon and test it out. Whenever you need more money just kill yourself and more hostages will appear.

That should be all you need to know. You can download it right here. If you have any comments or suggestions, make sure to email me at shingy@telefragged.com

Counterstrike Beta2.1 | @ 15:35 | shingy

Well, just a couple days past the release of the second beat version of Counterstrike, a new client and server update has been released. Some of the changes in this release are:

* added new version of cs_assault (compatible w/ hlserver.exe)
* added scientist model for hlserver.exe (sans ventgibs.mdl)
* added assault's proper sky
* fixed telefragging (cs_alley will no longer telefrag)
* fixed dropweapon
* fixed those spurious 'player joined' messages
* changed Ak-47 price

The patch weighs in at 1.6 megs and can be from right here.
