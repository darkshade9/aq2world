Here you will find replacement weapon models for Action Quake2.
M16
Modified M4 model by Muz, then modified (fixed + full vweap) by [-ZeUs0-]. Comes with vweap for most official models, a ground model, and working skins.

SuperHawk Magnum
A very cool model that replaces the sniper rifle. The poly count is a wooping 1400 polys. The animations are great too. Has full Vweap support for all official AQ2 models.

Tommy Gun
Oddjobs very cool and great idea for a model. The model is a little big but still looks good. Some animations look weird. Tommy gun replaces the M4.
