Action Quake Edition
 is an extended version of Action Quake2 1.52 with new features and bugfixes.


    Six new % variables for

    • name of enemy within sight
    • weapon of enemy within sight
    • position of enemy within sight
    • last killed player
    • your own position
    • position within your sight (pointing)

    14 new commands for

    • player map voting
    • player kick voting
    • ignoring of any message of specific players
    • local heard player sounds
    • postion determing and naming system
    • punching!
    • player on screen menu for voting and ignoring
    • onscreen help

    and

    • optional team friendly fire
    • new "tourney" game mode
    • detailed setup for player map voting
    • temporary banning
    • camper sounds
    • detailled setup for player votings
    • sounds within the menus
    • autodownload option for local heard player sounds
    • showing map with most votes at the end of the round


    - no more "out of ammo" bug
    - switched off infravision on dead corpses
    - no more "player died" bug
    For a detailed description and new options please take a look at readme.txt
