19th February 2000 (=DIE=Tempfile)
With some delay the new version 1.24 is finally out now and fixes a few bugs.

And now to something completely different. I have decided to stop working on Edition. Because I was the last remaining developer, Edition's development is now more or less finished. No more updates. No more support. Perhaps if some evil crash bug appears, I will take a few minutes to fix it, but I won't be doing any other work.
In the past few months, I have lost a lot of interest in programming Edition, and now I lack both motivation and time to continue the project. Additionally, some private factors contributed to this decision, but that does not belong here.
I would like to thank all of those who have contributed to the project for a long time. Those are especially Kerstin and PG BUND, and all of those who dedicated a bit of their spare time to the project. The ones who created .pg files, the ones who helped us debugging, the server ops who chose Edition and everybody else who I did not mention in this list. Without you, the project would have been impossible to realize.

Signing off for good,
Benjamin aka TempFile
