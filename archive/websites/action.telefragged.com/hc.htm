Weapons:
 Handcannon 12 Gauge
HC12 Technical Specifications
 
Caliber 12 Gauge
Ammunition Feed Hand fed 2 shells per shot
Barrel Length about 5 inches
Overall Length about 8 inches
Width about 4 inches
Height about 4 inches
 
    AKA Mister 12. This is a very close combat weapon only, don't even bother if they are more than 10 feet away. However, if they are within 10 feet, its hard to miss. This weapon fires dual 12 gauge shells that barely fit in the length of the barrels. The blast is quite powerful, and can clear out a small room or hallway with ease. However, you have to manually reload between every shot, making it harder to use.
    A favorite among Action Quakers, this weapon, if used right, will propell you to the top of the charts. The simple strategy is to charge your opponent, dodging his bullets, place Mister 12 against their forehead, and blow them out the 3rd story window. Suprisingly easy to do so, considering the hell of a knockback this weapon has.
