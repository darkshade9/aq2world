Weapons:
 M26 Fragmentation Grenade
M26 Technical Specifications
 
Body Cast Iron
Filler Flaked TNT
Fuze M204A1
Weight 26 Ounces
 
    Sometimes the old tricks are the best tricks. A fragmentation grenade will give anybody nightmares, if you are too close, or even NOT so close, you'll probably get ripped to shreds by its shrapnel, not to mention blown way into the sky.
    Difficult to avoid, and easy to use, this is a favorite of most players. Just use it carefully, the blast radius is larger than how far you can throw it!
