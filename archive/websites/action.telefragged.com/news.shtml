Suislide : suislide@telefragged.com

The new messageboard is up, thanks goes to Fex for hosting so long and Shagg for taking the role on now.

Also, iAQMD is a very cool program and will help people keep track of their maps. I recommend every Action fan check it out.

On to less positive news.

I have decided that my personal work on AQ2 will be minimal to none from now on. I may update this page from time to time if something truly important happen but the vast majority of news will go to The Action Half Life main page . I will still also answer some of the AQ2 related mail I get, though I hope that some of the more general questions can be sent to fan sites and the messageboard. If you have something that needs to come to me then don't hesitate to send, but for generic questions I would appreciate if you try others first as there are many good fan sites that are run by competent individuals who can answer your questions.

This in no way means that Action Quake2 is dead or even dying. With the release of the source we have seen a bot, multiple spin off mods, ctf versions, single player, and many more. The Guild is still kicking with new optionals, etc. all the time as well and the entire mod itself is still kicking and growing. This simply means that my personal involvement in these processes, already laxing, will be almost none.

I know that some of you were looking forward to the final pack I had spoken of but I will no longer be working on it. If Fireblade comes up with the motivation to make a final update then I will support that decision, but that is as far as it goes for official mod releases.

This is my own personal statement and all official sites can remain running as they currently are. I have weaned myself out of their processes already and my leave should have little or no effect on their ability to operate. For general news however, I recommend that you go to a fan site such as action world, etc.

There will also no longer be any distribution by magazine or any other non net form as in the past it has only served to upset exactly those people who it was intended to assist. All future distribution of the mod will be from this page's current files and anyone who decides to mirror the files electronically and unaltered. The only exclusion to this would be any current distribution I have no control over. I can only hope the rest of the team will honor this as well.

This mod has been in the making for nearly two years and has seen more time and effort from myself and many others than any project I have previously been involved in. It had to happen sooner or later and it is happening much later. For all practical purposes it is mature and bug free. This mod is Officially done.

With that in mind I want to thank everyone who has been involved in making the mod what it currently is. From the first players so long ago to the current community at large. All of the webmasters of the official and fan sites have my gratitude. All the server ops, without whom the mod could not be played over the net, also deserve a large thank you. The skinners, mappers, texture artists, modelers, and everyone who has ever submitted an idea or content that has been used in any manner helped shape this project into what it is today have my gratitude and best wishes. The sponsors and entrants to any contests I have held were and still are well appreciated. Our code maintainers both past and present also most certainly have my thanks. Siris, Gudlyf, Fireblade, Zucchini, and anyone who I am forgetting (or misspelled). The largest thanks, however, goes to all the members of the A-team who worked with me on this project. I consider them friends and have many fond memories because of them. I also want to thank everyone who ever tried to make any good person look bad. The world would truly be mundane without conflict. It may sound cliche but I also thank id for making the engine that made AQ2 possible.

I will be working on Action Half Life exclusively now, and I hope to see many familiar faces there.

Almost forgot.. The source code is still there so all the mods of AQ2 are encouraged to stay with it. I wish you all good luck.

If I don't see you playing Action Half Life or around the net then I want to say good bye.

Suislide : suislide@telefragged.com

Those of you getting tired of not being able to hit people in the head when they duck, and the various other bugs in Action Quake2 are in luck. The 1.52 servr dll is finished and on the ftp servers. It is server side only, so you do not need to download it unless you run a lan or net server. Grab it off the server files page. I do recommend that everyone read the text files though. I receive emails asking about things that are covered in the dll readme very often. If you do not wish to download the zip, you can find the readme here and the changes here .

Here is the release text direct from Fireblade:
When upgrading your server to 1.52, be sure to read the first item in the CHANGES file (if not the whole thing)...you should change your "dmflags" to be 256 on a normal teamplay server (otherwise you will have friendly fire turned on).

We'd like to thank the people who made this release possible, including our beta testers: JimmyHustle and Clan AR, Lurker and the Norwegian Whalers, and OvErR|De (with the NetNITCO teamplay server); and the people who made available patches for cool new stuff that is incorporated into 1.52: Eek, Azerov, Mikael Lindh, Black Cross, William van der Sterren, Hal, and probably some others I missed (sorry).

For anyone who uses a Mac and wants to play Action, your in luck. There is NOT a server dll port of Action for the mac but I have it from multiple sources that Action Quake2 will work with the Quake2 port for the mac. What this means is you can connect to a server and play just fine, though yu can not run a server of your own yet. Our source is on the server files page, so anyone who is willing to port the server dll to the mac shoul grab it off there and let me know. Once I get a working dll for the mac I will post it up for public download along with the others. NOTE: I am looking for an unaltered version of Action, not a port with changes.

The CGF bot is very cool, even in it's first early beta form. The little bastards are mean too. If you haven't tried it out yet, I recommend that you do. This is no ordinary bot. It is more along the lines of an advanced single player AI with scripted scenarios. As a matter of fact, there is a page dedicated to housing everything related to the bot, and it already has quite a few new missions to play around with. http://surf.to/cgf_resource_site . I recommend anyone who has played their fill of the default missions go there and grab some new ones. The face off one is cool if you have my gold akimbo skins from the Actor's Guild and the right player model (you will need both guild packs I think).

The Action Player model contest is still running and I would really like to see some good entries come out of it. Click on the CONTEST link to the left.

Since there is a new dll and there will undoubtedly be a lot of new downloads, I want to inform all new people of what all the Action veterans already know. The autoexec.cfg in your action directory (when you first download the client zip) is my own personal one from a LONG time ago. PLEASE either delete or edit it. At the very least change the name. There is a file called configexamples.zip which will help anyone who wants to make their own.

Clanspot closed a while back and I left the button up so that anyone who wanted to see their goodbye ,essages could do so. However, it has been long enough and I am taking down the button. I will be putting the action spin offs site's button it it's place as soon as they make one. I want to thank the Clanspot staff for their effort and time invested in the Action Quake clan scene. They were around since very early on and I am sure even the people who did not go to site must realise the large part they played in creating that scene.

For example, I have been using ICQ since it's first month of release and I have always HATED two things about it. 1)that annoying fog horn, which I delete every time I redownload icq and 2) that STUPID flower, which is worse than ever in the splash screens of today's versions. Now the splash screen is bigger, but is only really thin lines so doesn't get in the way at all, pus it repeats the words splash screens suck over and over. Is that spiffy or what?! So to make a long story short, what I want to do is start a new section under optionals over at the Actor's Guild and start accepting, making, and distributing these .exe wizards to turn programs into action themed programs. Almost anything is possible. Imagine netscape skins (like winamp sins for netscape), or changing the interface to a game (as long as the game is the right kind of .exe), shrinking, removing, or changing annoying splash screens, replacing an ugly program's graphics with action themed ones, or the buttons etc. A fully decked out clan customized version of icq with the little pansy flower replaced by a sniper crosshair and the rest all themed in action wit your clan logo in place of the icq text on the button. The list is endless. Anyways, I find the prospect extremely cool, so if you don't then just skip this link and ignore my ramblings.
