    A Heckler and Koch shotgun... how could you go wrong? This M3 Super 90 shotgun fires 12ga rounds that will punch a hole though anything (including that damned vest) it hits, but keep it for close and medium range combat, this wasn't made for sniping. Holds 7 shells, and you have to load each shell individually (using the reload key) from your stash of shells. Only have 2 shells in there, but think you might need more for the fight in the next room? Throw a couple in there, and go in blazing!
    An old favorite, it is quite possibly illegal to make a FPS without a shotgun. Ours just happens to kill like a real shotgun, you take every pellet and you won't have a chance to shoot back. The knockback is significant as well, so use this weapon with care.
