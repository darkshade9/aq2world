WORLD DOMINATION AQ2 TOURNAMENT 11/30/98  
Exhumist writes:

"We are ready for Round 2 of the World Domination Tournament.  We held a very successful Team Fortress Tournament that ended a few weeks back, and we're ready to move on to AQ2.
This tournament is set up like a sports season.  Every clan will play 16 matches before elimination.  It looks to be a fun tournament where many clans can get together and play meaningful matches, without worrying about being out again in a week."

Anyone interested should go to http://www.gibbed.com/tfwd to get more info.
