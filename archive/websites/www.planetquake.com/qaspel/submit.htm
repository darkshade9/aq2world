Demo Submission Guidelines
Updated 2-17-99

You want to submit a demo? Okay here's what I'm looking for, keep checking back because as my needs change I will be updating what I'm looking for.

I need to know who gets the credit for the demo, what mod it is for, what map it was on, and any other info for the brief write-up on the page. I don't watch all the demos but if I hear something sucks then I will check it out, if it does suck, I will cast it from off my site!

Current Needs: Action Quake 2 Team demos, LAN Party demos, tournament match demos and anything else that is cool!

Demo File Size: Under 1MB zipped, if you want to use the max compression then by all means go for it!

Other Requirements: Lots of action, good demonstration of movement, attacking, defense (where applicable) and strategy/tactics.

Preferred Perks: Stunts, secrets and other stuff that is done "on the fly."

"So what do I get out of it?" You're name in lights, in other words, the credit for doing the demo and the chance for people to see how much you can kick ass!

Send to: qaspel@planetquake.com.
