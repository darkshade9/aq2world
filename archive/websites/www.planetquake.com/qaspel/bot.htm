NOTE: All Z-Bot demos are sent to me in the confidence of knowing that their authors' names will not be revealed. Personally I think that Z-Bot and other such cheats suck, but hey, if you've got a demo I'll post it!

Read the readme.txt file to learn how to play the demos, if you already know how to do it, then go play the demos!
