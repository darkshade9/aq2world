How to play Assassinate The Leader (ATL)

Assassinate The Leader (ATL) is just like ETV. Each team has a "special player" (a Leader) who must "volunteer" for the round to start. This is simple elimination. Find the other team's Leader and KILL HIM!@#$ Meanwhile, protect YOUR team's Leader from getting killed.

One team kills the other team's leader. The other team kills your team's leader. As with ETV, if you are a Leader, your screen will flash at the beginning of a round and the "Leader" picture will flash in the right side of your screen:


Leaders generally wear black suits:


You get 10 points/frags for killing the other team's Leader. You get 10 points/frags for surviving a round as a Leader. If you are a Leader and kill the other Leader, you get a whopping 20 points/frags. Also, players get 3 points/frags every time they defend you by killing an opponent close to you--when you, as a Leader, kill ANYONE you automatically get the 3 points/frags for "defending the Leader." Neat, eh?
