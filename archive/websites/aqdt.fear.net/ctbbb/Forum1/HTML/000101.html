If you read the documentation that came with the server pack, you will see some mention of the 'viewpos' command, which is very useful in finding your coordiates.

As far as making maps goes, it depends on what kind of map you have. If you want a map with capture zones, then you may want to include the "info_flag_team1" and/or "info_flag_team2" entities as locations to pickup or take the flag/briefcase. You may also want to structure things so one or more teams has a defensible "base" area.
