In AQ:Edition (mainly European servers), %K is for the last person you killed.

In AQ:ETE (mainly North American servers), %K is the last person you HIT, NOT killed. So to use it as a "death message" indicating who you killed, it would not always be accurate.

I could shoot someone in the foot or do 2 damage on a kevlar vest shot and %K will say I hit them. But it is definitely not a kill.
