RAS = Remote Access Service. something about adminning remotely, which you don't need.

open up admin tools->services and stop the services that relate to remote access. in fact, turn them to "manual"

while you're at it, go to arstechnica.com, their NT4 tweak guide, find the one that tells you which services you can shut down, and do so. more stable, less overhread, more memory, and possibly more secure.
