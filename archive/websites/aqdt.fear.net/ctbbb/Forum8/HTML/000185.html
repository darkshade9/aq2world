Directions from Toronto:

Take highway 400 North to Barrie

Turn left off Exit 98 (Bayfield St, aka Highway 26 West)

When the highway forks just outside of town, take the right fork, it turns into highway 27 which is the road you wish to be on. Basically this amounts to going straight, but some people got lost at this point last year.

Drive to Elmvale (~20 min)

At Elmvale, turn left (Hwy 92)

Drive ~8 min to Wasaga Beach, look for water tower. The paintball field is at the base of this tower on the right side of the road.

Sunshine Park is what you are looking for, it about 200 meters past the paintball field, just after the water park. You will see a yellow sign that says "Sunshine Park" about 20 feet in the air on the right side of the road. If you see a big IGA supermarket on your left, then you have gone too far.
