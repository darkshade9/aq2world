Reporting a Match or Filing a Complaint

Use this form to report a match result. As with the registration form, it uses email, so if you do not have your browser setup for email, please manually send me one at i-cheng@uchicago.edu with all the information requested in the form. Thanks.

If you fail to fill out a required field, I'll get back to you as soon as I can notifying you of such.

No, once you hit "Submit," there is no page where the browser takes you. Just hit Submit once, and things will be done.

To find your tournament round number, look on the brackets at the column you are currently in. Look at the top of the column. Right above the name of the map is a value; for example, "1", or "C4A", or "W3", etc. That is your tournament round number.
