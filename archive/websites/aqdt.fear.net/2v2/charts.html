TOURNAMENT RATINGS

Here you can find the TOURNAMENT RATINGS. Basically they are generated in the following fashion:

You receive 16 points for every adjusted win. This is a match you played and won.

You receive 10 points for every win the administrator gave you. This was most likely due to the inability to locate the opposing team and your effort to demonstrate that you did in fact try to contact them.

You lose 5 points for every adjusted loss. This is a match you played and lost.

You lose 8 points for every loss the administrator gave you. This was most likely because you were AWOL during the time you were scheduled to play the match.

If I canned you from the tournament, your rating is automatically -100.

In the tables, the "WIN" column is your total wins, "LOSS" column is your total loss, "PLAYED" is how many matches you've played, "FORCED" for how many matches the administrator decided for you, "CANNED" for if I canned you, and the rest is described above.
