Tournament Format

The tournament is a double-elimination tournament consisting of matches between two-man teams in 16-team divisions. No single-entries are allowed. The winner of the divisional Winners Bracket (undefeated) will face the winner of the divisional Consolation Bracket (one loss) in a single-elimination division championship match. Division champions will square off in the final tournament championship single-elimination bracket for the ultimate championship title. Anyone can compete, regardless of ping and connection. The only restriction is that you must agree to the match rules.

 

Match Rules

1.) Match format will be a single-map elimination. Whoever wins 15 rounds first wins the match. The map to be played depends on where you are in the tournament. For example, all first-round tournament matches would be played on urban, all second-round tournament matches would be played on kumanru (these are just hypothetical examples. The map schedule will be released with the brackets.).

2.) Finding a suitable server is the responsibility of the parties involved, unless the administrator already tells you that he has prepared a server (e.g., for the championship match). The match will be played on a server that both teams agree to. If you cannot agree to a server, have both teams contact me with preferable times and time zones, and I will arrange a server for you to play on. However, I can be only so accomodating. Matches simply must be played. Therefore, if you play on a tournament-provided server, that match is automatically legal, barring exceptional circumstances, such as the other team confessing that they cheated, or repeated overflows or disconnects. The recommended tournament mod is Action Quake: Tournament Edition, by the AQDT. However, it is not required. You may play on 1.51 servers-- however, I cannot guarantee you that the other team will not be using unlimited chasecam to their advantage.

Additionally, only North American servers are allowed. Sorry to all the Europeans out there, but it just has to be this way. Additionally, you must be accomodating towards the Eastern Time Zone.

5.) Matches must be played by their deadline. Scheduling matches is the responsibility of the parties involved. If there is a dispute, the tournament administrator will force-schedule a match at a time he believes is accomodating for both teams. If you do not show up for the force-scheduled match, you will receive a loss. If you are unable to contact the other team, notify me, and I will attempt to contact them for you. If the other team does not respond by the deadline, they receive a loss.

6.) Rules against camping:

Camping is the act of using of a chokepoint unfairly to your advantage by staying in a vantage point that affords you an opportunity to shoot without the enemy having a fair chance to shoot back. Holding a defensive position is not camping. Fleeing from a pursuer and leading him into a trap is not camping, even if the trap is setup in a chokepoint. Camping is forbidden. The tournament administrator reserves the right to make full judgement on what is and what is not camping in any given case based on evidence presented. In lieu of sufficient evidence, rulings will come down in favor of the accused. The administrator recognizes that certain maps have blatantly "campable" positions. Upon review of the maplisting the administrator has decided to make the use of the following areas on the following maps illegal:

actcity2: the "secret" room (one with the kick-able entrance), as well as the bottom of the elevator shaft in the casino, as well as the high ledge where a spawn point is located (the area with a clear vantage point of the intersection, and where you must fall down on top of boxes in order to come down without taking damage.) If you spawn in this area, you must come down.

Additionally, the following areas strictly fall outside the definition of camping, as determined by the administrator:

urban: The roof of the highest building
cliff: Any position that has a clear view of the ladders.

Please do not file a complaint about any party using those areas to their advantage. If you feel you have a legitimate complaint that a party is using a position on a map to their advantage such that you have no chance of winning, I recommend two things: a.) you get to the position first, or b.) if you feel that it really is totally unfair, file a complaint.

All championship rounds will have an official representative from the tournament present to warn participants to move along, so no need to worry about those. It is recommended that you record a demo of the match or take screenshots if you plan to file a complaint about camping.
