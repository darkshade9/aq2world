Action Quake: Espionage Scenario Scripting Guide

 

Submission Rules

In order for your script to be released, it has to meet the following general criteria, that anybody should be able to figure out, but that deserve to be put down in writing:

  • Choose base locations with fair or similar vantage points. e.g.: ctbcity, 1 spawn point in each base, similar number of base entrances, and the flag is easily guarded/easily retrievable. (It would suck to play a CTB game on ctbcity if a flag was in the karlsson room)
  • One-flag Capture Scenarios should be similar. e.g.: actcity3 is a good example of a map with bases that have similar vulnerabilities, vantage points, entrances, etc...
  • Scripts that have very different sorts of bases (e.g., maybe one on a hill, and one down below, to create sort of a "defend the hill" scenario...lame example, but you get the point) should mention this in the submission form. These scripts should not be used for clanmatches, but just for fun's sake.
  • Avoid ATL scenarios, unless it's really something funny and creative. One ATL is not too different than the other; don't really do it unless you think of some cool "theme" that warrants a new ATL scenario.
  • Use skins and models that most everybody will have; preferably something from the Guild's modelpak or the AQ2 Clientpak.
  • Be aware of new options coming out for scripts, such as the ability to control the number of weapons and special items selected, new objectives, and new respawn options.
  • Be sure to include the filled out form in the zipfile with your script. It's really a pain not to have those.
