Q3DPDuel (Beta Build)
This is my 1st map to make it into a form I feel is suitable for release (nearly). This map just requires final playtesting to make sure there are no small glitches, niggles etc. and that the item placement is correct.

This map's sole raison d'être is for 1 on 1 tournament play with a human opponent. Although bot support is included, don't be surprised if you see them doing incredibly stupid things.

Bearing in mind this maps intended purpose, I have tried to concentrate on providing good flow, gameplay and action. I have also striven to make this map run well on most systems and so I have cut back on unnecessary eye candy and the use of hardware intensive shaders. Of course my target audience generally turns off all of these things anyway ;-)

Please download this beta version and provide me with feedback, either good or bad (constructive criticism preferred)
