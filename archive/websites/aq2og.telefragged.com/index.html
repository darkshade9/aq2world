Sunday, April 11

Gangsters

Gangsters is out. Server and Client can be downloaded at their page. I donno how this is going to compete with Espionage because I havnt tried out Gangsters yet but AQ:E is simply amazing. Specially Escort the VIP in Hostage2 with my custom Backstreet boy scenario :)

The 4 Maps that are used with Gangsters are Teacher, City, Avenue1, and Blockwar. These have special entities added to the code with location message. %L will report your location and %S will report the enimies you see.
Example: bind "say_team I'm %L, enemy spotted %S; radioteam aqg_company" will output "I'm outside the Police Station, Enemy spotted on Police Station Roof" and let everyone hear "we got company" (example from the map City).

AQ:G also comes with the Velvet Jackson model with AQ2 Vweap. I'll also be updating the Marine VWeap with AQDT Briefcase Vweap.
