News by Mloclam 7.17.00

I'm alive! Contrary to popular belief, I am still around, I've just been getting a lot of sleeping done recently (15+ hours a day) so other stuff tends to fall by the way. However, I have now finished my beginners guide to creating aliases and scripts in AQ2. This will help with a lot of basic and advice about what to do and how to fix problems, I hope it helps. Check it out here

Work is coming on well with Black Monk and his 'Uberpack', containing every official AQ2 map. He and the AQ2 Map Depot have agreed on what maps are official, something that was causing problems, and Windows testing has finished. All that remains is the Linux testing then it's ready to be released. Last time I checked, Monk was still looking for a Linux tester. If you're prepared to load 170 or so maps and note down any problems, drop Black Monk a line to see if you can help

There's quite an important news post concerning Action Half-Life, basically in a few weeks all work on AHL will temporarily be put on hold while the A Team releases the first version of Action Unreal Tournament. Then work will resume on AHL and it will have dives and stunts once again. Basically, Valve's Half Life 1.1.0.0 patch caused a lot of problems for AHL and other mods, the A Team is planning that by the time they finish AUT Beta 1, Valve will have fixed a lot of the problems and work will resume on AHL once again
 
Also on the AHL front, recently the whole community has got itself in motion making things for AHL so they can survive the dark months when the A Team is working on AUT. People are modelling, mapping, skinning, making graphics, all kinds of wonderful things. Look out for new maps on the AHL Map Depot in upcoming weeks, but the main place you'll notice activity is on Haven O' Frags, the official source for AHL player models, weapon models, weapon skins and oh so many other things, much like the AQ2 Actor's Guild. Releases are being made daily of new models and graphics, I'm amazed at the work being done. Recently there has been a new modelpack full of excellent weapon models and I must single out LPlasma's recently-released  incredible Desert Eagle model, I highly recommend you use it. I've even had a few things released myself, go look

Something I forgot to mention a while back, the first version of Action Quake 1 has been released. No, I'm not kidding, Action Quake 1, officially endorsed by the A Team too. Digital Nomad has been slaving away hard at this, unfortunately it's currently lacking servers but I strongly recommend you check it out still

What's that I hear you say "Action Quake 1, eh? What a crazy idea, what's next, Action Doom?" Well, now you mention it... Yes, it's true, there is an Action Doom. Quamosity is leading the Action Doom team and there is already a downloadable version. I played this myself with Doom Legacy (A Doom patch that makes the game truely 3D and supports 3dfx graphics cards), and I was amazed. Be sure to check this out
 
Well, I think I've covered every Action mod except one, unless a certain {CoW} is making Action Pacman like rumors say, except Action Quake 2. Ahh Action Quake 2, what can we say about the grandpappy of Action? Well, quite a lot. Firstly the Actor's Guild, the official source for replacement models, skins, sounds etc has closed it's doors and will no-longer be accepting new submissions. The site will still be up, though, so you can get anything that is currently there. I'd like to thank all the various people who have staffed the Guild over the past few years, Gigolo, Hammer, ActMarine, S'kebe and the others I've missed for their hard work and dedication

Also on the AQ2 front, the Action Quake Development Team has released version 4.3d of their amazing Teamplay Edition. It's like regular AQ2 but better, there are many features that simply votes, ignoring players etc not to mention fixes for popular cheats such as the M4 script and 'weapons farming'. It's incredible, I urge any and every server admin out there to start using this, after a quick read of the readme. For some reason the main page hasn't been updated, so I'll link directly to the files. Grab the Windows version or Linux version as appropriate and take your server to the next level

One last AQ2 thing, check out AQ: Espionage, download the client pack and grab a quick game on DaBoyz AQ:E at 209.201.80.121:27950. It's the greatest AQ2 variant ever, introducing lots of excellent scenarios and goals to liven up even the dullest of maps. Thanks go to Kramed for running this server, it's pretty-much the only place I play now

Having written all this, I think I'll grab a few of those 15 hours of sleep I need
