NEW GUY HERE!
I've been reading the AHL and AUT forums for some time now just to keep up on release progress and for entertainment, you people can be pretty funny at times. After reading this whole thread, I finally felt motivated enough to register and put in my two cents.

I don't have any "new" map ideas but maybe some alterations on those already mentioned and approval for those ideas that I like.

Swamp - If this was more of a wooded bayou, like from Gator McClusky's world, this could be a tense map. From trudging thru the thick undergrouth on land (not knowing who's lurking in the shadows) to wading chest deep in the dark green moss covered swamp water (not knowing if someone might be swimming in for the kill from below). Maybe an old run down Cajun shack with a rickety old pier stretching out into the swamp ready to collapse from age and a rotting half sunken aluminium rowboat tied alongside.

Marina - With rows and rows of boat stalls loaded with mid sized yachts and sailboats, this has potential for both ambushing and sniping. If the stern of a boat in one row was close enough the stern of another boat behind the first, you could run along the edge of one and long jump to the other for a quick evasion or to get a better line of site. Missing the long jump would force the player to swim to a boat with a swimming deck or a boarding ladder, not all boats contain these off the stern.

Mall - I once worked as a mall security officer (no laughing) in Houston, TX. The mall, which was simply a long/straight two story mall with large department stores on each end, contained a carousel in the middle of the food court. This carousel would be a visual obstruction taking away the opportunity to snipe from one end of the mall to the other, unless the sniper is on the second floor. I've also notice that several mall maps are missing the plain grey concrete access corridors located between the outer wall of the mall structure and the back of the stores. These allow access to the loading docks and the interior of the mall. These corridors would provide the same purpose as an under ground tunnel in other maps.

City Park - This is an idea for a map I had a while back (too bad I'm not a mapper). Sunset, concrete walkway, park benches, light poles overhanging the walkway, sloping hills and moderately thick woods on either side of the path and a large duck pond (ducks not required). This map could be two separate maps, one for sping and one for winter. In the winter version I think it would be cool to be able to run and dive for the frozen pond in hopes of sliding to the other side for cover. And if you don't make it... well, now you have to WALK to land, running won't get you anywhere, which leaves you in the open longer than you really want. The spring map would have leaves on the trees creating large shadowed areas to hide in and the unfrozen pond would still be usable. I think a city park map falls right into the Action theme. The good guys and bad guys almost always do their underhanded meetings in the city park.

Sorry about getting carried away with the discriptions. I just thought these ideas shouldn't be overlooked.
