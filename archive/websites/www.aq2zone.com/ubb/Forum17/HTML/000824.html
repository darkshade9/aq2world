Since every newbie comes to every new forum with "all these super ideas" and stick a smilie with sunglasses in the subject, I though I should do that too.

Of course there will be this "new element that will revoluotinaze the FPS genre" in AUT, the Team always talks about now and then, but here's my shot anyway.


Both teams have one randomly selected 'Hero'. If the teams are uneven, the Hero in the smaller team will of course be much more powerful. (like 100 to one )

Being the Hero, makes you able to do crazy things that are so common in action movies, like rush in a room full of enemies and slaugher them all with well aimed headshots. Now you prolly think: "Hey! This is supposed to be a realistic!", but hey, isn't this supposed to be action?

To be more precise on the Heroes...
- You are generally more healthy, fast and your aim is better. And you can do the greatest stunts.
- If you are hit, the one who shot at you will suffer some penaly, like slower reload, so you can crawl off to bandage yourself. (of come on... how fast those snipers reload when they hit the hero... "Hmm... the bastard didn't die... Gotta reload... Now I reload... Reloaded. Hey! where'd he go!")
- Heroes don't die from headshots very often, do they? Of course a headshot death is always dramatic, so: Headshots are counted as chestshots or something, _unless_ you've lost enough health already.

Of course when two heroes fight, there will be no penalties or suffering from head-chestshots (What? You mean that our hero-sniper would miss!?). The health and abilities would of course be there in order to make more interesting fight.


And now it's your turn to tell me, "Yeah. Good ideas, but there's already been a lot of discussion about a system like this."

No. I'm not a cynic. How did you get the impression?
