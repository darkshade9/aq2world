Well there are skeletal systems and then there are skeletal systems.

The skeletalsystem avilable to mod authors in HalfLife is quite different from what is availble to mod authors in UT. In some ways the UT system is superior and in some ways the HL system is superior. To make things even more complicated to follow the skeletal system in UT that is availble to mod authors does not have all the possibilites as what is availble to full licensees (obviously if you think about it).

However the nature of the UT skeletal system availble to plain mod authors however would make it quite difficult for a non-expert to distinguis between a vertex animated model and a skeletal animated model even in-game. If one could see a difference then its quite possible the different would be that the vertex animated model had better deformations.

The reason one wants to use a skeletalsystem is not so the model will look and even nesseraly to move better. You want a skeletal system mainly for the interaction between the models and the world and other models. Skeletal system or not it is still up to the artists involved to produce models that look good and animations that makes the models come alive on your monitor.
