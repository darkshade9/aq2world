S.F. LAN Party 2k:
As you may or may not know already, Tor (Chuck/Charles) and myself (Echo) are trying to organize a fun LAN party during the last week of March. This party will take place in San Francisco, at Club-i (http://www.club-i.com), a cyber-café where Tor knows the owners/managers. The facilities are good, they have computers, and an overnight staff for multi-day parties. Here is the relevant information for the time being:

Date = Monday, March 27 - Tuesday, March 28.
Times = Probably from late morning on the 27th to late afternoon/evening of the 28th.
Cost = $40 per person if you bring your own computer.
$60 per person if you use the Club-i computers.
The cost already has overnight staff factored in.
(The more that come the lower the prices.)
Stuff to bring:
1. Sleeping bag
2. Pillow
3. DVD's/Movies (big screen TV baby!)

5. Any other games we might want to play other than AQ2. Check with Tor to see if Club-i might already have the game.

1. A working NIC card (network interface card)
2. Action pre-installed and ready for use.
3. Your material marked so the shit don't get mixed up. (yes I said shit =P)
