I went outside the other day, after nearly getting burned to a crisp by this round hot thing in the sky, and found myself at a mall, in the arcade. First place I went was in front of the HotD machine, to see if I could try playing it akimbo, but one of the coin slots was broken. Turning around, I see this machine with an MSG-90 attached to it.

So I wander over to the token machine, which surprisingly accepted my mangled $1 bill, and went back to the machine with the sniper rifle attached. Thankfully all the arcade junkies are playing Marvel vs. Capcom 2, so Silent Scope is free. Putting my coins in, and hitting the start button, I pick "Story Mode", and get three choices, one of which is a highrise kinda thing, where I have to support some flatfoots on the ground from above. So I enter the game after skipping the intro, and this 0wns! I put my left arm under the barrel, dismayed at finding a big hole in the machine where my elbow would normally rest, and took aim at my distant targets, nicely highlighted by little inverted triangles. Peeking through the scope, I see enemy snipers taking aim at my buddies down below, and after noticing they look and move exactly like the aqmarine/urban model, minus the pack o' smokes on their helmet, I took my shots. Damn, first one went over his head. But my first target didn't notice. He fired downward. He didn't get a second shot.

After my aim steadied, and improved (I've never actually looked through a scoped rifle before), I took out the snipers on the roof, and then their comrades on the ground. After the next mission, a freeway chase, ended with the time running out, and being distracted by an urban "gangsta" saying this game was "tight" (Such amusing faunae, these arcade dwellers), I got some more tokens, and picked a different scenario.

This time my target was dragging a hostage by the neck across a football field, full of football players. Fighting the urge to take shots at the jocks, I sought my prey from my perch on the lip of the stadium, and spotted him. But try as I might, I couldn't keep my scope on him long enough to take a shot. His lackeys on the field unfortunately made short work of me as I futiley tried to kill their zigging and zagging boss.

Despite losing, and losing badly, I had a fucking BLAST playing this game, and would recommend playing it to any who would brave the Outside. It 0wns j00. It's a pity the American version of the PS2 Silent Scope won't have a rifle peripheral. Perhaps if I win the lotto or something, I'll buy the arcade machine. Just one without holes in the cabinet.
