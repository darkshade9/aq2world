Made Man 		posted 02-11-2000 05:13 PM            
Looks like you have 4 brushes with errors. You're getting the 'coplanar plane' error, which is usually caused by problems during carving and/or vertex manipulation.

The brushes are at or near coordinates:
(-129, -784, 0)
(-129, -784, 37)
(132, -785, 37) and
(132, -785, 0)
they all have the texture CHROMEBAR.

Try deleting these 4 brushes and rebuilding them. You may need to make them out of 2 or more brushes each if you can't get the shape you want without getting errors. Remember every brush has to be a convex hull, i.e. no 'dips' in the surface.

-- Dr Bob
