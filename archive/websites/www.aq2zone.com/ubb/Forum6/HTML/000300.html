I just wanted to clarify this for everyone.

W_Poly, also known as World Poly
Consists of EVERYTHING in the map that is based on brushes withing the .bsp file.
Such as floors, walls, skies, func_door, func_button, func_wall, ANYTHING you made into the map by hand, or by prefab *shiver*

E_Poly, also known as Entity Poly
Consists of EVERYTHING spawned from the map and engine that is based on .mdl files.
Player models, weapon models, ammo boxes, monsters, projectiles, etc


Thank you again, and good night

Actually. Its not that obvious and people do do it.. (Me)

It stems from worldcraft meanings. If you create a door or a train, you convert it to an entity.
