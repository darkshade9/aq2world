personally i cant stand wally
here's how i do my custom textures
1.) open it with MGI Photosuite (i like that better than adobe, because it doesnt try to automatically 'fix' your images)
2.) resize it to multiples of 16x and multiples of 16y
3.) save as 8 bit bmp
here's where i bet im gonna lose you)
4.) open quark
5.) open texture browser
6.) edit > import (copy files)
7.) after you import mytexture.bmp, edit > copy object as > Half-Life Texture
8.) edit > paste
9.) there you go, a half-life texture in 8 easy steps.

The reason i don't like wally, i couldn't get high quality stuff to NOT be converted to low quality crap. either old ass version or user error, either way, the method i just explained is what i live by
