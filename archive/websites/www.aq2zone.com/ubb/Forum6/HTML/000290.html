When designing a level, use software mode for checking everything....

r_speeds seem more meaningful (as it tells you how many are drawn / how many weren't drawn / how many in total).

also you can use these two nifty commands at the console.

r_draworder 1

draws the screen from furthest point to nearest point. This allows you to see exactly what is being drawn.

r_drawflat 1

draws every polygon as a flat polygon. This probably would have allowed you to spot the problem that Goat noted about your stairs, as you would see that the the side of the house would have been split into polygons exactly with your stair case.

Generally if you can see everything in your level, or there are loads of small polygons when they should be big ones then you know you have problems
