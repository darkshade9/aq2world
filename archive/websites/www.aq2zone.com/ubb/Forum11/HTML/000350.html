Try this:
Face your objective.
From a standstill, jump forward.
In midjump, rotate your view to be 90 degrees from where you want to go.
Continue to jump without letting go of the forward key.
Make sure that you use your mouse to change your angle so that your final jump is as close to the edge as possible.
I recommend practicing this on the ground until you are able to run at ungodly speeds.
