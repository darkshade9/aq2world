It depends on what you want to make. If you want a regular logo (2 color) then it's easy.

Just open an existing logo (in your half-life\logos\ folder) and edit it. They have to be 8bit greyscaled pictures. And leave the size alone. (standard is 64x64)

You can also make colored logos and change the size around a bit (but there is still a limit). This is kinda hacky, and is a lot more work, but there's a tutorial here.
