Spraypaint - you have to bind your key. Go into configuration, controls, then bind it. It's that easy. Then you can go to customize, and then choose your name, model, and spraypaint.

kewl jump kick thing - you can only do that when something or someone is in front of you. If you see people jump kicking each other, what they are doing is just jumping in front of each other, and the coders of AHL have designed it to where all you have to do is jump in front of someone, and it makes it a jump kick, and it hurts the other guy. You can also jump or jump kick (whichever you want to call it ) in front of a window, which will break it, cuz you just jump kicked the window.

Hope you understood that.

Jump Kick = Jump when something is in front of you.
Jumping = jumping when something is not in front of you.
