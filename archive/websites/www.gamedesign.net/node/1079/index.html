HL2CTF Map Spotlights

Home » Content
Submitted by Andy on Fri, 2005-05-06 18:24.

Part Two of the HL2CTF Map Spotlights that will prelude HL2CTF Beta 1.4, but only just as more news from Patrick Flanagan, about the intended release of Beta 1.4 on Friday the 13th can be found at the end of this article.

This Spot light features CTF_LambdaBunker by atrocity:



  1. What is your map?
    CTF_LambdaBunker
  2. What inspired your map?
    The lambda bunker from HLDM, Great map, and I figured if I changed it up a little bit made it HL2 themed it could work for HL2CTF.
  3. How long did it take to build the map?
    A long time, It Started as a HL2DM map and then when I was invited to the HL2CTF sides of things I converted over and kept working on it. It's still not 100% to where I want it.
  4. What do you wish you could have added to the map, or had in the map but was removed?
    Even more detail then put in it, other than that I'm rather happy with it, just a few layout tweaks.
  5. What is your favourite Game / Mod and why?
    Half-Life/2 and Firearms for the mod, I started all of my level design with HL and now I'm expanding to HL2 and Firearms was the only mod I followed, I've always been a fan of modern day war.
  6. Do you have any tips about your map, or inbuilt gimmicks the player has not found?
    There is an old "Blue Print" in the bunker that has an old layout on it, check it out.

Please don't forget to visit us at www.hl2ctf.com for updates, and more information,
Thank You
Fired Okra Games

More news in from Patrick Flanagan

Hey guys,
I wanted to give everyone a heads up that the Half-life 2 Capture the Flag mod is planning to release our next version, Beta 1.4, on Friday the 13th! (cue the scary music).
Beta 1.4 will make the classic CTF game we all know and love even better, and also feature 4 new CTF derivative game modes to mix it up and add some variety.
We've also implemented a slew of new server options such as sudden death overtime and a mercy rule to help administrators better customize their game.

We've posted a media update on our website at www.hl2ctf.com that shows off some of the updated and new maps that will be included in Beta 1.4 as well as a general description of the new game modes.
We'll also be having a release party on our IRC channel (#hl2ctf on irc.gamesurge.net) that night at 8pm EST.
