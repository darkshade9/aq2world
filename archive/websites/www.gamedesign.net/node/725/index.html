Biopsy

Description: Like Andy, you could say I'm getting old in relative proportion to those currently filling seat at the major game studios. I've been immersed in this reality as long as I can remember. I'd start by saying before Quake III came along..

..but it's more true to start by saying before my SGI 320 came along.. I had little ability to take part in studying such wonderful arts. Without my baby most everything you see before you of me would cease to have existed. Many years ago I took part in the sacrificial rite of youth internship in Halifax NS. There I learned the magical mysteries of PowerAnimator V8 (Thats what Maya used to be called) and went on to work placement at Marshall Media Group in the penthouse of one fine structure downtown overlooking the harbor. Ah, those were the days, a room full of SGI's and a keycard, I practically lived there.
