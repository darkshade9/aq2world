Electronic Arts - Los Angeles QA Game Tester Job

Home » Content
Submitted by Method on Sun, 2005-03-20 19:03.

PlanetQuake posted a new job ad in their Help Wanted section. Electronic Arts - Los Angeles are looking for a QA Game Tester.

"We are seeking Tester candidates for a full-time temporary, non-exempt, non-benefited position. It is a requirement of the position that you are able to work forty or more hours per week (including nights and weekends as needed) playing video games in front of a computer and/or television for extended periods utilizing controllers or other devices."

Head over to PQ's Help Wanted section for more info.
