RUST Community
Submitted by skinmaster on Wednesday, November 10, 2004 - 01:11

Yes, It's a Community.

We at RUST are dedicated to gaming, to level and mod design and to people. You are what makes us special. You're the cream of the crop. You bring life to worlds of thought and bot fragging simulation. Without you, rust would have no reason to be what it is today and no reason to become what you help us shape it into.

We want to urge you to participate in any way that suits you. From the forums to the News. Dig around, if you see something that you feel is out of date or you can fill in a blank or fix an error, pm one of the mods/admins. You can tell who we are by our avatars in the forums.

Rust Forums

If you're a veteran of an application like GTKradiant or Hammer, keep your eye on the forums and help the newbs get directions. If you're a newb, spend some time in the books and gather what knowledge you can before posting silly questions. Don't be shy however to dive into new threads with anything you like. Beta test your maps, Show off your textures, models, skins, weapon models, anything.

We're working to make the forums easier to use, more functional and to stay up to date on the new editor releases, so that we may cover what's important to you. Thus we suggest you tell us what it is.

RUST Resumes

As a means to promote visibility for all our members, we at RUST have set aside an area for Resumes.
Rust Resume's is a prototype database, to list and keeping track of the latest gaming work by Level Designers, Models, Texture Artists and Coders.
The Resume Template will help you create your own RUST Resume and assist you in showing your work off to the gaming world.

RUST Design Competitions

RUST hosts competitions for best design, be it map/level, architecture, lighting or full map based on any of the games and editors we cover. Prizes vary per comp. Level design comps, model design comps, screenshot pic comps, you name it. If it's related to gaming or making games, We're ready to give it to you. All you gotta do is show up and compete.
