Movie
Posted by Andy on Thu, 09/02/2004 - 16:51

ID Software sold the rights for the original DooM movie back in the 90's... There was heaps of news articles like the one mentioned, I remember the headlines, "Game Geeks make a killing". The truth was that with a little clever marketing ID Software had sold the rights to a movie that couldn't be made.
The realitiy is that we all experience different emotions when we play..
That cannot be translated to script, and unless you are a game freak, the resultant movie will leave non-gamers feeling ripped off.
Don't believe me?
They have had 10 years to produce the film.... Nothing happened.
It may well have been a case of "Buy the rights" so no other company gets them.
This script for DooM 3 may well be the same?
