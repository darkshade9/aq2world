RUST Competition Winner announced !!
Submitted by Andy on Sunday, October 24, 2004 - 00:40

The compo guidelines asked the contestants to produce a screen shot of a map that would 'make' the viewer want to be there and all the entries exceeded our expectations.

The diversity and skill on offer proved without a doubt that there are some very talented designers out there. Unfortunately there can be only one...

First Place goes to natestah :

Click to enlarge (640x480)

More information about this entry here

Judges Special Recognition Award

These entries recieve a special mention for the quality of design and visual "yumminess".


Click to enlarge (640x480)

We took into account the difficulties faced by the designers with respect to the game engines they were mapping with.
Every entry had a lot going for it, so much so that I wanted to have a look at all of them but at the end of the day, even with weighting the score to assist older engines a choice had to be made.
I thank all of those who entered... Congratulations are in order to each and every one.

The next RUST Competition will be announced very soon.
