ModDb get's a face lift.
Submitted by Andy on Thursday, September 23, 2004 - 06:08

Mod Database V2 Update

After a year of development the time has finally come to unleash
Mod Database v2.
Whilst the new site will appear similar to the present one, it has undergone over 200 changes.
Each of these was implemented to carry this site onto bigger and better things.
Here are the notable updates:
  • New design (easier on the eyes)
  • Mod tutorials section (at last!!)
  • Support for multiple languages
  • Support for multiple templates
  • New super-control panel for ALL members
  • Better / more accurate search systems
  • New permission system (cater for more staff members)
  • New accurate mod rating system
  • Cleaner mod listing
On the 25th of September, the Mod Database will be updated.
There is a scheduled downtime here of 2 hours, however given that 25,000 images have to be updated and 1,100,000 database records have to be shifted about, this may take longer or something may go horribly wrong.
Rest assured there will be a massive party / gathering in #moddb on irc.gamesurge.net so join in for the latest news.
