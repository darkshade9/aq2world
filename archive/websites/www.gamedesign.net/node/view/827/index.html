6 New Skies for Quake II, Quake III and Half-Life.
Submitted by Andy on Monday, November 15, 2004 - 03:27

Kold has been busy working hard on some new Skies for Quake II with a new twist and new colours.
Quake II is not the only game they can be used in. These skies will run in Half-Life and Quake III. (Thanks, Miniwood)


Kold say's:

"My main focus on this new batch of Skies was to get more familiar with Terragen which is a beautiful program for making landscapes, and for many people like me , env's for popular games."

"I've also been playing around with making more out of the world places than just your average grassy plains or desert. I found some new techniques by reading lots of tutorials and learning from other authors to create something new and enjoyable among the gaming community."

"I was trying to make my Skies as different from each other as possible to create something new for the ever so fast mappers , above is what my new skies look like from the lf , bk , rt , ft views."

You can view & download the new Skies from:

Kold's website listed under "sky boxes".
