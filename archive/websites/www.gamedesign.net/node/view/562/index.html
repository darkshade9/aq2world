Rain in Quake 2

Chris "Kold" Stringer, has just submitted a new tutorial Quake 2 Tutorial on making rain. Chris said:

" ... Jester, a fellow mapper recently made this deathmatch map and despite the amazing brush & texture work .. one thing that caught my attention was the rain, now although Quake2 doesn't support such a feature without a engine mod, there is several way's to make it with one of them being animated (or flowing) textures if you will ... "

Check out Kold's Tutorial on making rain
