Multiplayer Mod's

To play these Mods on-line you may need the Steam multiplayer system:


 

Counter-Strike

One of the most popular Mods ever. Fight as a terrorist, plant bombs, stop the hostage rescue or play as a counter-terrorist and rescue the hostage, protect the VIP and stop them planting that bomb!.

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

Team Fortress Classic

The ever popular Quake mod has come to Half-Life!!
Choose from 9 classes, each with there own ability's. Work together as a team to capture the enemy's flag. Getting Fragged isn't a problem with TFC, the player keeps respawning until round end.

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

Day of Defeat

World War II has come to Half-Life.
Play as the Allies or the Axis, and pick from several classes to fight the enemy. One shot/one kill action all the way.

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

Natural Selection

Fight as a Human under the lead of a commander against the Aliens, or evolve as an Alien into a deadly creature.

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

The Specialists

Use the special stunts and powerups to crush your enemy's in this adreline packed Deathmatch Mod.

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

Half-Life Rally

Hop into a shiny new rally car and challenge your oppponents.
Hl-rally features amazing cars and maps!!

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

The Trenches

This Mod takes you back to the horror of the First World War.
Play as the Allies or as the Axis.

Review | Visit Website | Download


 

Hostile Intent

NATO forces are fighting against TANGO forces in different locations all over the world. This mod is for the "thinking" players.
It is not Counter-Strike. Visit the site for more amazing details. A Total Conversion Modification for Half-Life

Review | Visit Website | Download
