Vote For The Lost Temples!

Submitted by Method on Wed, 2005-07-27 16:03.
Many players, especially in Kingpin community enjoy Cypress Hill music. They not only did soundtrack for Kingpin: Life of Crime, but also recorded voices for several characters, including Jesus in Skidrow. "Cypress Hill's sound is exactly what we had in mind when creating Kingpin," said Drew Markham, president of Xatrix Entertainment. "The hard-core sounds of hip-hop are making the natural progression from the stage and big screen and into the interactive gaming industry."

If you want to hear more of Cypress Hill music, make sure to drop in on official DJ Muggs site and vote for The Lost Temples LP.
