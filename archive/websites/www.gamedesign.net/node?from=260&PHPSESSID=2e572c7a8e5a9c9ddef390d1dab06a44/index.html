Nvidia announces the NV40 Chipset

Submitted by Peeves on Thu, 2004-04-15 02:10.
April 13, 2004 NVIDIA hosted a LAN party in California USA revealing its new graphics card - Geforce 6800. Utilizing the new NV40 Chipset the Geforce 6800 delivers an amazing array of new features dominating every consumer based graphic card on the market today. The Geforce 6800 has amazing features such as support for Direct X 9's Pixel shader 3.0, 16 textures per rendering pass, 16x anisotropic filtering (with the Intellisample 3.0 technology) and more. For those of you who like games that use volumetric shadows (a.k.a. Stencil Shading) this is a must card for you to own, the new NV40 chipset has increased performance focusing on the shadow technologies used in games such as Doom 3, Far Cry and other games that utilize the volumetric shading technology.
