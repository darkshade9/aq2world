posted 03-04-99 01:04 PM CT (US)          
Ok to get the blue to go away make the ladder a wall entity and set the render mode to solid and the FX amount to 65 or whatever looks good to you. Those glow sprites do exist. Select the env_glow entity and set the render mode to additive and the Fx amount and color to what you like and in the model space type sprites/glow03.spr .Its actually 03 not 3. Get a pak file viewer and you can look at the list of different sprites.
