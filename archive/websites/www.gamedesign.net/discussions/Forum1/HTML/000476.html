You know the map boss1, at the end of the map where he stands on that platform, he's like a hologram, you can hurt him, how can i create a hollogram, can it be used in a dm map, or ra2 map, and how do you create that effect after the hologram disapears, all those little partilces, thanx

  
David Hyde
posted 02-17-99 09:08 PM CT (US)          
That's a monster_boss3_stand in boss1, a special entity that doesn't do much but stand around lookin' pretty until he's triggered and teleports away. If you want the same kind of effect you'll have to make your own model. Since monsters can't be used in normal DM play (w/o a mod like MapPack), the monster_boss3_stand can't be used in DM.

"Those little particles" are a target_temp_entity. See the tutorials for a description of all the effects you can use.
