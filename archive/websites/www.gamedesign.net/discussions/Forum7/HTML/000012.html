In the Research Lab in Quake2 SP, (lab.bsp), right after you enter the level to the left there is a plexiglass wall with several marines milling around inside it. I don't remember exactly how it was I got behind it the first time, but when I did, I found that there was a small passage way that led out of the room. After crawling through this passage way, I came out into another room that has another plexiglass wall in it, with one dead marine and one living one. On the plexiglass, written in blood, is "kill me," and this message, in this format, pops up:
A.H.D.S.S.I.B.H.
bjjc

I have no idea what this means; I was wondering if anyone else had ever seen this secret or has any idea what this message means. I don't remember exactly how to get past the plexiglass the first time, so I just used NOCLIP.
