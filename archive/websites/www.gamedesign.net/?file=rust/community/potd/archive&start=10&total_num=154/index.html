Real Bosnai
[ 2002-11-05 | sent in by Mr Damage ]

This is a new DM map for Kingpin. Bosnia is a conversion of a SoF map made by Turbo . Its a small to medium sized map set in a Bosnian village during the recent war there. The village is right in the middle of the warzone as is evident by the bombed out buildings and the sounds of a battle raging in the background. I have used a combination of the original SoF textures and Kingpin textures and included the original custom sky and custom sounds. You can download it from - http://downloads.kingpinforever.com/bosnia.zip
