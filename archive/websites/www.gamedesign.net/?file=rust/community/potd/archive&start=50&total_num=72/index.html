Water Vending Machine
[ 2001-09-30 | sent in by Gusher ]

One unique approach about using unreal editor is the ability to import real world pictures onto your models. Look here...a simple cubic brush resembling a water vending machine. The picture and related textures were made and optimized in Photoshop. Once imported to Unreal Editor you can manipulate it in many angles and sizes...conforming to the surroundings of your new level. This approach works very well on such realistic mods like Strike Force, Tactical-Ops, or Infiltration.
