Reflection
[ 2001-09-07 | sent in by Koert van Kleef ]

This is an experiment of mine te create simple reflections in the halflife engine. The trick used is an inverted 2nd version of the scene below the water. When the water is in motion this creates an acceptable idea of reflecting water. Of course this could easilly be used for mirrors as well except that players are not reflected. For any comments or ideas for improvement please feel free to contact me.
