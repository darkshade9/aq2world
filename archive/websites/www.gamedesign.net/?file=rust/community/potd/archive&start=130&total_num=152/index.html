Cottage
[ 2001-09-13 | sent in by Mauro Frau ]

Above all I built this wooden cottage while testing some lightmaps effects for my development team's 3d engine. It worked out pretty well... under the skybox there are four external omni lights, they pass in through some roof’s fractures and through the broken intermediate floor creating some cool beams around the scene. The planks on the windows are aimed to the same goal ofcourse. I also tried to simulate radiosity by supporting those sun lights with five internal dim and coloured omni lights. Textures are all done with Adobe Photoshop.
