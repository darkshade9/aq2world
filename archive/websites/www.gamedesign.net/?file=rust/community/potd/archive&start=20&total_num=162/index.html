Storehouse Hassle
[ 2003-03-27 | sent in by -Cat ]

This is a new Bagman map for kingpin. The theme of this map is set in a small storage place and a section of a industrial site. The weapons ammo and armour are plentiful and equal to both sides. This is a medium sized map with 28 spawn points. There are nice, fast routes from the base to the cash pit. If you are quick and skilful enough, making your way home may be easy enough, those who aren't, good luck. You can download it from - http://downloads.kingpinforever.com/team_shh1.zip or you can download it from my site - http://www.poisonville.net/cat/
