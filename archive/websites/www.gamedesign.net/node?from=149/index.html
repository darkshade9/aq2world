Scholarships for Game Design courses.
Submitted by Miniwood on Saturday, April 10, 2004 - 23:15

The Art Institute of California, San Francisco is currently offering scholarships to High-School seniors who can demonstrate talent.
Scholarships are available for students pursuing degrees in Graphic Design, Media Arts & Animation, Game Art & Design, Visual & Game Programming, Fashion Design, Interior Design, Multimedia & Web Design, and Fashion Design. Scholarship awards are based on the quality of projects submitted by high school seniors who demonstrate ability and commitment to studying in the school’s programs. Students may qualify for one of up to nine full-tuition scholarships of up to $70,000 each, or one of a variety of partial scholarships depending on submissions.

Q3MAP2 - Version 2.5.14 released
Submitted by Miniwood on Saturday, April 10, 2004 - 22:49

Ydnar announces the new version of Q3MAP2, the best compiler for Q3A and games based on the Q3A engine. New features include:

- New: -dirty mode courtesy of RaP7oR (dirtmapping/ambient occlusion)

- New: q3map_skyLight code using more uniform angular distribution about the sky hemisphere

- New: q3map_alphaMod set N.N

- New: q3map_alphaMod scale N.N

- New: q3map_alphaMod volume - applies all other q3map_alphaMod directives to each vertex inside a brush textured with this shader, allowing large faded scrolling fire shaders, waterfalls, marquees, explicit dotProduct terrain blending control, etc.

The Wadfather is back
Submitted by Miniwood on Saturday, April 10, 2004 - 22:41

The site that calls itself 'The Ultimate Collection of Public Domain Graphics for Game Developers' has relaunched with a new look and easier to use interface.

The Wadfather is a great source for graphic files for Half-Life, Q3A and Unreal and currently boasts:

* 475 environment maps
* 184 texture collections with 17902 textures
* 62 desktop wallpapers

The latest update is a new texture set by evillair based on the classic Quake 2 textures which you can download in Q3A, Half-Life & Unreal flavours.
