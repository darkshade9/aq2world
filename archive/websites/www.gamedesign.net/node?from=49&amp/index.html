Contributing Content
Submitted by Jherax on Saturday, July 17, 2004 - 21:43

So, you want to help contribute editing-related content to RUST | Gamedesign.net... great! Here are a few guidelines for content submission if you are interesting in writing an article or two to be added to our editing handbooks.
1. Check it once, then check it again!

Be sure to double check your article or tutorial for spelling and accuracy errors! Nothing's more disappointing then getting halfway through a great tutorial and releasing that you can't go from one stage to the next because the author forget a step.

2. Stay focused!

Make sure your article/tutorial stays focused on your topic. Although it may seem great to announce to the world about your new pet dog, Fido, somewhere in that article of yours, don't forget that someone looking to learn three point lighting techniques probably won't care whether you named the dog Fido, Pluto, or Dips--t for that matter.

Major League Online Mapping Contest
Submitted by mazemaster on Saturday, July 17, 2004 - 02:33

Major League Online, a Counter-Strike gaming league, has announced a mapping contest with a cash prize for the first and second place winners.

Prize for winning:

1. First place: Map is used in the league map rotation. And a choice of: $100 or you get use of 2 months of a 12 man private server.
2. Second Place: Map is used in the league map rotation. And a choice of: $50(if a map is worthy of second place) or you get use of 1 month of a 12 man private server.

The deadline is August 20th at midnight PST, so if you have no other map plans for the rest of the summer you might want to enter the contest.
