King of the Server
Submitted by Neuro on Wednesday, September 29, 2004 - 16:33

If you are a Quake2 player you may have heard of the mod KotS, short for King of the Server. Once this mod was immensly popular and arrested many real lives for more then 3 years. If you wish to know more about this mod please visit the official website.

The ORBIT KotS team is going to try and revive the mod one last time by reseting the statistics so everybody will start out level 0 once again.

Now is the perfect time to jump aboard. No 'impossible to kill' level 10 railers, but fair play for all and a chance for a shot at the title.
