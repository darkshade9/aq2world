How to set character's moral?

MORAL

Moral is effect willingness to fight until death,
attack unprovoked, etc. Used with characters.
There are 7 kinds of moral.

MORAL_RANDOM 0
MORAL_COWARD 1
MORAL_HAPPY 2
MORAL_NORMAL 3
MORAL_AGGRESSIVE 4
MORAL_BESERK 5
MORAL_HEROIC 6
MORAL_PSYCOTIC 7

 Example use: select a character and open Entity
inspector.

Key moral
Value 5

 Moral also used with ai_boundary and ai_territory.
