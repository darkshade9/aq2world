Official RUST Competition ( 1 )

Level Design Competition

The Idea:
Take a screenshot of a level you build from scratch. The idea is to show us your mapping and editing brilliance.

Game Type:
You can take a screenshot from any game that is covered at RUST.

Guidelines:

  • Only one screenshot of your map/area is needed.
  • Posted Screenshots shouldn't be bigger than 320*240.
  • Must have the original editor and compiled map file.
  • Post your entry HERE .
  • Date the competition closes is the 15th October 04.
Judging:
Points will be awarded based on the "WOW" factor.
  • Do I want to play this map?
  • Does it excite me?
  • Is it visually brilliant?
Remember to have the .map file at the ready, as the winner will be required to produce it.

Note: RUST Staff are not allowed to enter.
