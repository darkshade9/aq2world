BIOGRAPHY:
I became interested in mapping way back in 1998 while stationed overseas. It started with Quake 2 and after returning to the US in 1999 I eventually got around to finding a download for Q3Radiant. I worked with it for about a year before I realized the amount of options in Q3A was just too daunting to work with and during that time, I stumbled across a small Quake 2 community, Backshooters, who warmly welcomed me into their house. I had the benefit of learning more about Quake 2 mapping from mappers like Maric and Sven. Over the next two years I built my skills using QERadiant and began releasing maps in 2001.

- MAPPING INTERESTS:
As I stated above, I have loved working with Quake 2 and am looking forward to working on some Doom 3 maps.

- SKILLS:
QERadiant, GTK-Radiant, Wally and PhotoShop

- WORK:
To date I have released only one level for Quake III Arena which wasn't all too popular but a lesson in and of itself. I have also released six levels for Quake 2, some of which have been modified for the Awaken 2 mod for Quake 2. I have been involved in beta testing some code for the Awaken 2 mod but my heart is in mapping and that is where I suspect it will stay.
