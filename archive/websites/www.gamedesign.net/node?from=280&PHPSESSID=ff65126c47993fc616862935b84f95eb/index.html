Clan Recruiter

Submitted by Kingpin_Xatrix on Mon, 2004-03-08 23:12.
For those of you who want to join clan or recruit players for your clan you can visit Clan Recruiter.

So, what is Clan Recruiter? Well in online and LAN games, players group together in teams called ‘clans’. Clan Recruiter is a website that helps clans to find players and players to find clans. Currently, clans recruiting players have to use forums, in-game chat and word of mouth, which is hardly ideal. Clan Recruiter changes this by providing a central place where clans can go to list themselves and find available players, while free players can pick a clan that suits them. Clan Recruiter is all about communication and community building within an accessible, user friendly website.
