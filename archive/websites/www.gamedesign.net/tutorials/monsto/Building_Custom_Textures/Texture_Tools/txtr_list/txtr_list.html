Texture Sites

Here is a list of links to sites with textures, skyboxes, etc., that I have collected from various sources.

I have not visited all of these sites, so some may be obsolete. Others may have photo-realistic textures that are inappropriate for tiling (at least without some editing), or may have to be resized.

Also, I can't vouch for the quality and/or accuracy of the tutorials.
