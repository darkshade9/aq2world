Done!

If you wanted to try your hand at textures, I hope that you can do that now without spending a weekend reinventing the wheel for one lousy texture. I've tried to give you enough knowledge to strike out on your own.

Believe it or not, this one little panel could very easily turn into a fambly of 10-15 related textures. Several panels similar to this one, plain tiling concrete (with and without brown tiles), maybe with more/less scarring. Different colored tiles (red and blue comes to mind) could be achieved with basic color manipulation. It could get outta hand.

Now I can hear the email now ``Dude that's the long way to do that" and stuff like that. Yes I know photoshop has a butt-load of plugins and effects that you can use, but the free tools might not have the 2 click plugin. And besides, doing stuff by hand gives you a bit more control over getting what you want.
