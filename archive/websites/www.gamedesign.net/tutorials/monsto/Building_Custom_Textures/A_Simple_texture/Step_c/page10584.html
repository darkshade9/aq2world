c. A touch of detail

It's cement, but it's quite plain. Let's add some pretty but functional tile to give it that special touch.

  1. Add a new layer, make sure it's over the concrete. Call it ``Brown Tile" just for organizational purposes.
  2. Open up greenbrown[1].jpg into a separate image
  3. Select a section for copying over.
    For simplicity, making it an even number of tiles wide will let you use small sections of the txtr in-game without breaking tiles in half
  4. Copy the section over and scale it into place

I chose a 8 x 4 tile section, scaled it down to fit 64 vertical and copied it twice. I could very easily have used 2 sections of the same sized tile or whatever.

Also take note of the placement. It will let you use the upper half by itself without the pretty tile, or you can use the tile as a border between the bottom half of concrete and a completely different texture above.
