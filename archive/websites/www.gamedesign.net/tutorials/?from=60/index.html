Action Unreal Tournament Beta 0
Submitted by KaMiKrAe-Z on Wednesday, September 15, 2004 - 01:10

The Action Unreal Tournament (AUT) mod, created for Unreal Tournament 2003/2004, was released only a few days ago. AUT is the sequal to Action Quake2 (AQ2) and Action Half-Life (AHL), both popular mods in their own rights. While the team that created AUT has only one orignal member of the A-Team that brought out AQ2, the aim of the mod remains the same: to re-create the feel of your favourite action movie, without having to pay for a ticket!

Hostile Intent v.1.3.5. Release
Submitted by pepper on Tuesday, September 14, 2004 - 10:06

The Half-Life Mod:
Hostile-Intent has released there latest version: v.1.3.5.
You will find more information at the Hostile-Intent Website.

They have many new interesting features like bullet penetration and new walking animations.

This mod also features the ironsight system, which means you have no crosshair to aim but you have to raise your weapon to aim and fire, And it requires a lot of teamwork and strategy.

This is in no way a counter-strike rip-off.
