December is a good month for game dev jobs.
Submitted by skinmaster on Monday, December 27, 2004 - 16:20

I'm the type of guy who's constantly scouring the web for references and such. One of the thing I look for is game dev jobs or game industry jobs. That's site's listing positions offered by game dev companies.

CGTalk's Studios hiring Forum is probably one of the best for this as it's updated with more positions, more often than most and it's frequented by more actual paying offers than the rest. A lot of game dev job lists tend to be full of unpaid mod design offers and such.. Rather annoying to sort through when you're looking to get paid for your time. Read on..
