pic of the day - how-to


All pictures must be game editing/level editing related.

quickguide to sending me pic of the day

send me:
  • large pic in .JPG format less then 100 KB in size.
  • Small Thumbnails should be under 20kb
  • Small thumbnail .JPG of the same Pic 150 x ## in size. height doesnt matter. Only width.
  • text file with title of pic, description, name of author, and email address of author.
    • Send these files to picofday@gamedesign.net.

    Pictures will be ignored if they do not conform to these rules.
