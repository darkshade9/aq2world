RUST REBORN: Forum Merger Completed
Submitted by G.A.R.D. on Monday, August 9, 2004 - 03:47

On the 20th of September 2001 disaster struck the game mapping community when the RUST Forums of the day were hacked. In the last few days of the old WWWThreads Rust Forum some idiot was constantly posting as other people. The_Relentless was less than impressed when the hacker posted as him in this thread. Before long the hacker just went crazy with numerous stupid posts. Finally the Administrators of the day removed the old forums and a few days later created new forums, which are still very much in use today. Unfortunately at the time it was deemed too complicated to merge the old forums with the new forums, as the SQL’s were just so different. Thus the hacker won, and RUST lost many of its best contributors. Many of whom moved on or just left the game making community.

That was the worst day in RUST’s history. Now a new team of RUST Administrators and Moderators have rebuilt RUST. Have resolved the SQL problems and brought back the old lost RUST Forum’s and merged them with the new Rust Forums. Thus completing RUST.

At Gamedesign.Net we never forget anyone who contributes to RUST. Every post that sheds light into the art of MOD making is valuable. Every post, tutorial, news story is part of our history and will be preserved. We are not just any game making map community we are the BEST!

It is with great pleasure that I inform you RUST is now whole again, and like the Phoenix from the fire we will get stronger. We never forget those that contribute. Those that inspire us to be the best mappers, modelers, game designers. So In tribute to everyone who is contributing or has contributed to RUST’s history, we have decided to post the names of the all time best RUST contributors (in order of total posts). If you know anyone on this list … tell them RUST’s says thanks!
