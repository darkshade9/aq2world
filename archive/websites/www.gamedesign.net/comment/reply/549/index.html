RUST Resumes

The Online Mappers Resume Database - Developed by RUST

Rust Resume's is a prototype database, for listing and keeping track of the latest gaming work by Mappers, Models, Texture Artists and Coders. The database is designed to show the best of your work, provide links to all your material and help people find you.

To add yourself to the RUST Resume's Merely Fill in the following, provide any images of your work you would like to display, and Register to RUST (This merely ensure's your email address is current and you are who you say you are).

To post your Gaming Resume here click the add comment below, and you will be added in the next few days. Post any additional material, such as pictures to Oirfid or Gard

Every six months RUST will email to confirm the contact details, and work profile is upto date.
