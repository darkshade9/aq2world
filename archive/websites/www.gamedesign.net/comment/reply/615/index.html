Action Unreal Tournament Beta 0
Submitted by KaMiKrAe-Z on Wednesday, September 15, 2004 - 01:10

The Action Unreal Tournament (AUT) mod, created for Unreal Tournament 2003/2004, was released only a few days ago. AUT is the sequal to Action Quake2 (AQ2) and Action Half-Life (AHL), both popular mods in their own rights. While the team that created AUT has only one orignal member of the A-Team that brought out AQ2, the aim of the mod remains the same: to re-create the feel of your favourite action movie, without having to pay for a ticket!

The frenetic gameplay is enhanced by a realistic damage system, so there is locational damage (a headshot is nearly always fatal), and a need to bandage in order to stop bleeding when hit. There are a small but well balanced assortment of weapons ranging from the Robar bolt-action sniper to the lethal Tonto throwing knives. These are complimented by a similar array of items such as a laser sight to improve accuracy, and a kevlar vest for added protection against those pesky bullets heading your way. AUT allows for a number of different game-types including Deathmatch, Teamplay and Last Man Standing.

Two distinctive features of AUT are the ability to dive (ala your favourite action hero) - which allows you to fire at your enemy while moving quickly and presenting a smaller target to him; and the akimbo weapons system not previously seen in an Action mod. The akimbo system lets the player arm themselves with a weapon in each hand. Any combination can be used but there are trade-offs for having such an edge over your opponent...

As with all beta's a number of bugs have popped up already - but don't let this deter you from giving this excellent mod a twirl! The AUT team are probably already working on their next version and you can expect this ti be another excellent quality mod from this talented team.

If you're interested in AUT head over to the AUT page at www.aut2k3.com and don't forget if you want to map for AUT try Rust's very own Unreal Forums for great tips and hints.
