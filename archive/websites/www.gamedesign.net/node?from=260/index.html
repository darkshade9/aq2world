Dan Koppel's interview

Submitted by Method on Mon, 2004-05-17 18:40.
I asked Dan Koppel from Gray Matter Studios (Kingpin, RtCW) a few questions about Kingpin, level designing and importance of sketching the level.

DAN KOPPEL'S INTERVIEW

RUST: Please tell us about yourself and how you got into gaming business?

Dan Koppel: I love playing games and making games. Currently I'm the Design Lead at Gray Matter Studios. My first game I worked on was Quake 2: The Reckoning. I also worked on Kingpin and Return to Castle Wolfenstein. Before all that I released a few of Quake 1 maps into the community, back then user made SP levels were much more common.

Halo Custom Edition & Halo Editing Kit Released

Submitted by Miniwood on Thu, 2004-05-06 09:28.
The Halo Custom Edition and Editing Kit allowing users to add their own content has been released by Gearbox Software with little fanfare and no announcement on either the Gearbox or Bungie websites. The Custom Edition is a standalone multiplayer add-on for the Halo: Combat Evolved PC game which adds support for user-created content. You'll need a valid CD KEY from the original game to run it. The Editing Kit comprises all the tools you need to create your own maps and content for the game. The add-on and the editing kit is available as one 170Mb download from 3DDownloads. DOWNLOAD HERE
