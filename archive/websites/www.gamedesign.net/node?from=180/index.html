DOOM 3 spec requirements

Submitted by Method on Tue, 2004-07-20 21:27.
Todd Hollenshead, Id Software CEO, said that DOOM 3 will require: A 1.5-gigahertz Intel Pentium 4 chip or AMD Athlon 1500. 384 megabytes of memory. Two gigabytes of hard drive space. An nVidia GeForce 3 graphics card or better; or an ATI Technologies 8500 or better. If your pc doesn't meet the spec requirements head over to Houston Chronicle to read on how you can improve your pc.

Major League Online Mapping Contest

Submitted by mazemaster on Sat, 2004-07-17 02:33.
Major League Online, a Counter-Strike gaming league, has announced a mapping contest with a cash prize for the first and second place winners. Prize for winning: 1. First place: Map is used in the league map rotation. And a choice of: $100 or you get use of 2 months of a 12 man private server. 2. Second Place: Map is used in the league map rotation. And a choice of: $50(if a map is worthy of second place) or you get use of 1 month of a 12 man private server. The deadline is August 20th at midnight PST, so if you have no other map plans for the rest of the summer you might want to enter the contest.
