PsychoStats Update:
Submitted by Gard on Tuesday, February 10, 2004 - 04:56

For those of you who don’t know, PsychoStats is a statistics generator for the game Half-Life, and also fully supports the Counter-Strike and Day of Defeat MODs. The statistic generator only gives the basic player stats and does not show team statistics and certain map based statistics unless the MOD is supported fully by PsychoStats (which is rare). The code is constantly being upgrade and this minor limitation will be solved soon (I’m sure).

Gold Llama Nominees Announced
Submitted by Gard on Tuesday, February 10, 2004 - 04:56

Well it isn’t the Oscars, but the new Gold Llama Nominees for 2004 have been selected this year, for recognition in great game movies. There are 12 categories:

Best Picture, Best Audio, Best Tech, Best Editing / Direction, Best Frag Movie, Best Non-frag / Trickjump Movie, Best Presentation, Breakthrough Video, Best Quake Movie, Best Unreal Movie, Best Counter-Strike Movie and Best RTCW Movie

All Nominees this year were picked out by the ESReality admins. The winners will be announced in a week. You can check out all the nominees at ESREALITY::COM.
