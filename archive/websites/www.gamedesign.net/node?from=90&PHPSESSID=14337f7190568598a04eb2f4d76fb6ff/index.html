Levelpedia.org Launches

Submitted by Andy on Mon, 2005-03-07 03:50.

This week sees the official launch of a new community database/website, designed to supply information about single player first person shooters levels.

Unlike other websites, Levelpedia.org is only interested in released files. No files are hosted, meaning costs are reduced to a minimum, hopefully ensuring its long term existence.
