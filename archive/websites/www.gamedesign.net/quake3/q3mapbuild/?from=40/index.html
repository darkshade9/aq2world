RUST launches Quake II Newsmail
Submitted by Miniwood on Monday, November 1, 2004 - 23:22

Quake II Newsmail is a monthly email rounding up everything that has happened in the Quake II community throughout the month. New maps, new mods, new servers; you name it, if it's anything at all to do with Quake II, then it will be included in the Quake II Newsmail.




The mail will not replace news sites and forums delivering up to the minute information but will provide a digest of the month, making sure you never miss anything that's going on in the Quake II community.
