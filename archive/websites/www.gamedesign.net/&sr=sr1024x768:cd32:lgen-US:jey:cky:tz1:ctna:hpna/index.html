Titan Skybox Texture

Submitted by G.A.R.D. on Tue, 2005-01-25 08:40.

I found a backdrop for a Titan skybox texture whilst reading the latest news on the Nature website. This is an image that us mappers can use for any level. Think about it ... you could be the the first to create a FPS on Titan. Mike Zawistowski has created a 3D image rendering of Titan that provides an approximate view of what Titan might look like. Based on the actual digital photos beamed back to Earth. The image was created with the Terragen software. I myself have used Terragen to create backdrops for maps, and it is very good software. I imagine that the guys at NASA were a little surprised to see a rendering of the Titan before they themselves had created one (and they call themselves experts!!!). Anyway, it is interesting to see software, that we use for mapping, being used to map a distant moon. I bet the first person to make a FPS level of Titan will get their name posted all over the web!
