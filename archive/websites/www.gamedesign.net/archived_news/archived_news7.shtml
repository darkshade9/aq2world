 Are you ready for Quake3 Arena Editing? (10:51) Updated by [ Fishman ] 

Got something really cool for yall today. Been working on them for awhile, decided to finally post them. Most of you know that Quake 3 Arena will ship a couple months down the road and that the editor Q3Radiant for it will come on the CD. Q3Radiant is a new version of QERadiant, an editor based on the original id level editor. QERadiant is a powerful editor but due to the lack of documentation not many people use it except for professionals. Well I have started a series of short tutorials aimed at teaching people the basics of QERadiant so they will be ready to edit Quake3 as soon as it is released. I am going to try and keep the tutorials simple and not get to the entity specifics of any game, though the tutorials will be for Quake2 since that's the game QERadiant supports the easiest.

I am going to try and post at least 2 new tutorials a week, which I think should be possible. Without further ado, check the first 3 tutorials out here.

Altered versions of QERadiant will also be shipped with Daikatana and Kingpin, so these are not just for preparing people for Quake3. One last point to make, I am not saying that QERadiant is the best editor, just saying that it will be the first to support Quake3. I am confident that quite quickly after Quake 3 is released other editors like QOOLE and Worldcraft will have Quake3 Support.

Post questions, comments, suggestions, errors, etc on the QERadiant Forum
