Unreal Tournament 2004: Editor's Choice Edition Planned For Fall
Submitted by RaVenous on Monday, August 16, 2004 - 23:30

After receiving many awards from various magazine and online publications, Epic announced that they will be re-releasing Unreal Tournament 2004 as Unreal Tournament 2004: Editor's Choice Edition.

The gameplay will remain the same as the original game. But, what is different in the re-release is the number of added extras.

The biggest addition to the game will be the three new vehicles available in Onslaught mode, the Cicada, the Paladin, and the Self-Propelled Mobile Artillery (or SPMA). The Cicada is two-man flying vehicle where one person can pilot while the other mans a belly turret. The new Paladin vehicle is a heavy tank that has both offensive and defensive capabilities. As for the SPMA, you can get an idea of what it is just by reading its name.

Citizen-132 is Recruiting
Submitted by oirfid on Monday, August 9, 2004 - 16:45

The FarCry MOD Citizen-132 is recruiting for the following positions on their team:

Sandbox Mapping : People with experience in FarCry level design. If you are interested, send us some examples of your work. Texturing and Static Modeling are not required, but if you can do that, send us some pictures of that as well.

Animation : People who can rig and animate characters and weapons. If you think you have the skills (don't be afraid even if you doubt your own talent, we look at every application), send us a demo reel or something similar.

Skinners/Texturers : If you can skin or texture characters or static models, show us some work. You won't be required to model either if you don't want, but we need some decent people. Show us some work of yours, either 2d or 3d.

Character Modelers : People who can model, as we could really use 2 more character modelers. Citizen-132 has many demented creatures that need to be done, so we need the extra help.

Citizen-132 is a Action/Horror mod using the FarCry engine. Read more about this project in our forums or at the Citizen-132 web site.
