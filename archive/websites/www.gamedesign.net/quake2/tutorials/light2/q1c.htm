The most moody of the shots. A store room, which IMO is very nicely lit. The lights on the top are placed between each girder, and cause them all to cast 'ribs' everywhere. The low lighting on the ceilings below help make the boxes cast their own shadows, which keeps this room relatively shrowded in gllom. And yet there are plenty of areas with plenty of light. Running around in here with 4 other people is a nightmare, very scary, and a hell of a lot of fun. Sniping in here is even better, since you can climb up to some of the crates and hide. Mind you, you're trapped there as well :)
