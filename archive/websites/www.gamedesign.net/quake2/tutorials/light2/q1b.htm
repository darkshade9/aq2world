Ah, Entity Lighting :) Plenty of it here. As it happens, this room is mostly sourced. The main light is above the walkway, casting a shadow beneath it, and the light on the right lightens these shadows. Again, the Shadows help contrast between the various positions of items in the room. The Door Frame really 'comes out' at you, and the slight shadows on each step of the stairs help them stand out more, and appear more real. I quite like this one ;) The shadows provide good places to hide from other DMers, if you are so inclined, and yet one shot will show you up. Nice.
