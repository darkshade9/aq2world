Alternative Lighting.

OK, so It's not the most pretty image in the world, but it does demonstrate some effects. Careful use of coloured entity lights creates a realistic effect, while still allowing for the interesting shadows. If you don't like the way they cut out, you could remove the lower lights, which are on very strong - they are small texture lights, which sometimes require ridiculously high lighting values - each of these has a value of 25,000, yet they don't really seem to add that much. They do, actually, but the effect is very subtle.
