Annual Come Back To Kingpin Weekend

Submitted by Method on Thu, 2005-10-06 20:57.
MrDamage of KingpinForever.com wrote:

"It's that time of year again, time for the 4th Annual Come Back To Kingpin Weekend. This year the weekend will commence on Friday 14th October and run through til Sunday night.

At KP4Ever we plan to have the following servers running:-

- Da Playgroundz Hitmen running 25 of the favourite Hitmen maps
- Detropolis running 25 of the latest maps released
- Classic Gangbang (KPDM1-5)
- Classic Bagman (running the stock BM Maps)
- Custom Maps Server running best maps of 1999-2000

Of course we want you all to turn up for some fragging but we also asking you to start spreading the word now on your ICQ/MSN/Email lists. Contact old teammates, friends, in fact anyone you know who has a copy of KP laying around to come along for a few frags on the Come Back To KP Weekend on 14-16th of October. If you all join in and spread the word who knows how many ppl we can get playing that weekend. Send a msg to everyone you can think of and tell them to do the same so we get through to as many ex-kpers as possible :) "
