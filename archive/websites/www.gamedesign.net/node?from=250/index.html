Human Head Studios Hiring

Submitted by Method on Mon, 2004-06-07 19:06.
Human Head Studios, Incorporated is looking for an animator to add to their collection of heads. This is a full time, on-site position with benefits for a project using the DOOM 3 Engine.

In order to be considered for this job, they require that you be able to demonstrate the following:

1. Thorough understanding of animation fundamentals
including posing, timing, and character weight.
2. Be able to rig character models for animation
3. Working technical knowledge of Maya Animator.
4. Willing to accept direction and work well under pressure.
5. Self motivated and a desire to work with a team.

DOOM Movie

Submitted by Method on Fri, 2004-06-04 19:02.
Todd Hollenshead (Id Software CEO) revealed that Warner Brothers, who had the right to the DOOM movie, passed on the option of making it. DOOM's movie license has been picked up by Universal Pictures now. The movie's script is based on DOOM 3, which is as stated by Dark Horizons will be shipped on July 15th.

Interplay out of business?

Submitted by Method on Fri, 2004-06-04 18:09.
After Interplay's financial problems, there was a few rumors that their office is closing and they're going out of business. Yesterday, GameSpot talked with the surprisingly affable Caen, who denied the end was nigh for Interplay. His proof? "You can see our stock is trading, so we're still here," said Caen. Raven Software is looking for a Technical Artist

Submitted by Method on Sat, 2004-05-29 12:54.
Rick Johnson updated his GameFinger plan with news that Raven Software is looking for a Technical Artist to work on the Quake 4. Technical Artist Position for Quake 4: Raven Software is looking for a Technical Artist for the Quake 4 PC title. We prefer a person with a strong understanding of the technical side of creating art assets, such as creating textures and world models, generating shaders, and proficient in scripting. We also prefer a candidate with strong drawing and painting skills and an ability to model hi and lo-poly models in Maya and/or Lightwave. Programming experience is a plus. A background with Quake 3 shaders, a familiarity with the Quake 3 Editor, and experience in animation and effects are also a definite plus, but not required.
