OWF Survivor:
SURVIVOR NEWS
THE TRIBES


Coming Next Tuesday!
OWF Survivor is a play off of the fWo's survivor, except it is only for members and it is practically run by the members. The grand prize for the last surviving wrestler is a shot at the World title, and a mystery suprise.

***The teams, and progressing news on OWF Survivor will be posted on this page***

Maybe I would like this...but what the hell is Survivor?
Well since you are one of the 2 people who has never seen the CBS hit, survivor, it was a reality show where people live on an island and have to "survive" the wilderness, but the majority of the show revolved around "challenges" and "immunity challenges" in the challenges they won something that the island couldn't provide, in the "immunity challenges" the two "tribes" competed to see who wouldn't have to vote one of their own members off. In OWF Survivor, we don't have challenges, just "immunity challenges" Once there was only 10 people left the two tribes merged, and voted each other off, and instead of fighting for a team for immunity, it was for individual immunity.

Rules of OWF Survivor:

1. The entire federation is split up into 2 teams, if you are a member of a stable, your stable will NOT all be on the same team, to make for more complex situations.

2. Every TUESDAY there will be a match between the two teams, after the result of the match, the loosing team must vote one member of their team out. They do this by emailing me with their vote. Alliances are not only allowed, but encouraged. On Saturday the one who is voted out is revealed.

3. When half the wrestlers are voted off the two teams go into one. Every Tuesday there is a battle royale for immunity, between wednesday and saturday everyone emails me their votes, you may not vote for the person who has won immunity (of course)

4. The last 2 people left are put in front of all the previous wrestlers and all the previous wrestlers vote to see who should win the title shot.

5. If you are voted out you don't get to vote anymore, except for the final vote.

Other Notes to remember:
-OWF Survivor has nothing to do with your angle as an OWF wrestler unless you want it to, it is all up to you, there will be no special OWF Survivor roleplay board, everyone must observe the wrestler in their natural roleplay scene.

-OWF Survivor is meant just to be a fun thing, it is not meant to make anyone feel bad, people vote based on strategy (if they are smart) and not on personal reasons, if you loose, no problem, that doesn't mean you are out of the fed, and it doesn't mean you will never get a shot at the World Title.

-I decide the two teams, they will be posted here later.

IMPORTANT!
If you are voted out of the team you ARE NOT out of the fed, just the chance to win the mystery prize and the World title, it is not up to me who gets voted out, so don't quit because of that, if you are smart you will form an alliance to ensure you don't get voted out right away.
