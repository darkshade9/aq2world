Rules:

These are the rules that you must follow to get in and stay in to the fed. If you don't meet these requirements you are temporarily or permanently banned from the Roleplay Board (I can do that, don't think I can't) hehe, but really, just try to follow these rules, I'm doing these to make you guys have the best experience possible in this fed, not to be a bitch.

1. All Wrestlers MUST be made up. This is big, I know that you guys might not fully like this, but trust me, it makes for 10 times better storylines, and roleplays, everyone likes a creative guy, even if they pretend to hate him, they secretly do appreciate you. If your character is made up you also get to bring him from fed to fed to fed and continue his life, trust me, it is a very rewarding experience. This same thing goes for stables, they must be made up. There is nothing better than having your stable reunite in another fed, it feels really rewarding. Also, you cannot take a real wrestler and parody him (aka Snott Hall...hehe Nano...) Remember anyone can say "cause stone cold said so" and drink beer, but if you have your own character the options are limitless.

2. Swearing rule: Not in subjects, but legal in roleplays, don't abuse this privilage, I can say if you are swearing too much, you get a warning, just please, you can swear but don't use it 3 times in every sentance.

3. The Prezident and Vice Prezident can do whatever they want in their cards. They can make twists using your characters however they want, you respond to them. But at the end of the card things will be the same as they were before they card, but we have permission to manipulate your characters as we see fit, especially if your character's plot is getting a little slow. If we do do something to your wrestler in a card, don't look as it as bad, look as it as an opportunity to advance your plot, we're just giving you the helping hand we think you need.

4. Don't whine. If you whine and cry when you loose you are on grounds for permanently banned. If you loose a match wait it out, you might loose the next one and the next one, but trust us, we know what we are doing, we are NOT dissing you on purpose, we have a plan (no, really, we do!) for everything we do, just trust us and wait it out, keep roleplaying your hardest and eventually it will pay off--BIG TIME. If you think you are a good friend of the Prez or VP and you loose and then whine we will seriously consider kicking you out, so please, do not whine.

5. This is more of a statement than a rule. If you win the world belt we will use Poser 4 to create a 3D representation of your character. Once we get used to the program everyone gets a picture of their wrestler.

6. Just because you think your roleplays are long, that doesn't mean they are great. If I get bored reading your Roleplay I will read it again, and if I still get bored, well, that's not good. This doesn't mean you will loose your next match, but it does mean that you gotta really do some grabass to catch up with your opponent.

7. No Racial, sexist, or Religious jokes. End of story. I shouldn't need to explain this any more. Trust me, creative jokes are totally appreciated, but racial jokes might be by some, but for me it sickens me, even though I am white. So don't mess with us here.

8. No Informing the Fed of your website (unless it is your wrestler for this fed's site) on the Roleplay board, there is a place to do this, and that's by emailing me and I'll put you in the links section. I will delete your message immediately and ban you from the board for 3 days. That means you might not get any more chances to roleplay before your next match or you only get one day to play catchup (or grabass if you ask letterman)

9. You get 2 wrestlers to control and 1 tag team partner. Temporary characters, girlfriends, wives, siblings, that won't last long can be used but don't get matches. If you wish to turn a temporary character to a real character email me and I will analyze the situation to see if your temporary character could truly add to the fed.

10. You may not beat up, hurt, or do anything to another wrestler. You can do whatever you want (if you rape, or do anything sexual to a girl that you control you are immidiately banned from the fed, no questions asked, but I shouldn't have to mention this (hopefully)

11. You may not suck up or ask a board member for an opinion on a certain thing, about the fed (we are open to friendly conversations not about the fed) If you try and sneak information out of us you loose your next match no questions asked.

12. You MUST have a sample roleplay in the join application, without one you will not get in, no matter who you are. 13. We do accept complaints, but if they are whiny then we will just forget them. If you can resonably complain, we would be more than happy to hear from you, but don't be a brat. That's about it. Sorry for sounding mean, I'm not a mean guy, if you know me you already know this, I just needed to get that out of the way, if you follow those you will be on good terms with me, whether you are a face or heel. So submit that damn application!
