Wrestler Info:
Height: 6'2"
Weight: 249
Eye Color: Blue
Hair: about 3 inches long and is dark blue.
Physical Description: Lowell wears a Kane-like outfit, with the colors black and blue. He has a build similar to Christian's, except a little more muscular. He's got a black goatee on the chin. He wears baggy black jeans and white Nike sneakers.
Hometown: Bangor, Maine
Status: Face
Entrance: The lights in the arena go out, leaving the fans wondering what's coming next. Out of nowhere, "Hello Time Bomb" begins to blast over the speakers at the same time there's 4 huge blue colored explosions on the stage where Lowell makes his enterence. Lowell walks down to the ring, he stands in the middle and throws his fist up in the air which is followed by blue flames coming up from each of the four turnbuckles.
Finisher Mover/Type: System Crash/Standing
Finisher Description: Lowell kicks his opponent to the stomach and gets him in powerbomb position. Lowell then lifts up his opponent up and powerbombs him to the mat. Without letting go, he lifts him up again, but this time grabs his throat and chokeslams him into the turnbuckle.
Background: Lowell DotCom was born on September 8th, 1978 as Magnus Lowell Gibbs. When he grew up, he didn't like the name Magnus, so he had his friends and everyone call him Lowell. He went through high school being one of the most athletic students their. He also maintained a B average. In his junior year he became fasinated with electronics and got his first computer. Since then, he always got the fastest computer he could afford. In college, he got the internet in his first month. He was always on it, so he earned the nickname "Lowell DotCom" from that. One day, he and his friend who was still a highschool sophomore got on it one day and came across a website dedicated to wrestling. They both laughed and thought about starting it up. They researched it and learned the basics. They tried out for an independent federation and made it. Soon they both quit school. Lowell and Chris formed an alliance known as NetCom, but were never a tag team. Their manager, on September 26, 2000, recommended that they tryout for the OWF. He left them after that and the two tried out for it.
