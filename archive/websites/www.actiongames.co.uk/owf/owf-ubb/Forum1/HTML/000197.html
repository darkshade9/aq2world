Author Topic:   Run away Angel... that's what you are good at
Night Crawler
Member 		posted October 20, 2000 07:29 PM               

A scene opens showing Dream and Night Crawler talking behind the scenes of a Friday night OWF show.

Dream - OK, we are going to go out there and address those idiots that there is no way tomorrow night Angel is going to pin you.

Night Crawler - I will...... destroy.... him....

The image fades away with Berry Barlett calling out:

Barry Barlett - Night Crawler and Dream... they are up next!

After a few commercials, the show returns to the air. Showing the full OWF arena. then the lights go out.
Night Crawler’s theme begins to play. The crowd quickly begins their boos.
The arena lights dim to a light red color. Dream steps up from behind the curtains. Followed by Night Crawler. The crowd's boos are getting louder. Dream shakes her head, looks at Night Crawler and then they both make their way to the ring, as Night Crawler's Online-Tron video is shown.

Dream and Night Crawler get into the ring. The crowd is getting tired from the constant booing, so they slow down, allowing Dream to get the microphone from the ring announcer, and to begin.

Dream - Alright, we will make it short and sweet, because we all know, that we don't like you. [points at the fans] Don't like us, and we don't like you.

The crowd boos again.

Dream - Oh shut the hell up!!!

The crowd continues to boo, Dream speaks over their volume.

Dream - Angel...

once the crowd here's the name Angel, the all switch their boos into cheers. Dream stops, and talks to herself. [But the crowd can still hear it]

Dream - ...My god, they are a bunch of yo-yos...

Dream continues with the speech.

Dream - Now, Angel, listen dude. Tomorrow night, Night Crawler and you, in the ring. Saturday Night Surge... Angel, Night Crawler is going to rip you into pieces.

Dream pauses, takes a breath and continues.

Dream - I saw your last, so called promo, Angel... very impressive. Is that the image you want to show to your loyal fans? An image of a run away, a man who can not stand for himself, a man who needs the help of his buddy to survive? Well guess what? After you step in the ring with Night Crawler. You'll be running away from two men.

The crowd does not like the way this is going, and begins to boo. Dream speaks over their boos.

Dream - Angel, tomorrow night, you have two options. One... Run away, save yourself for thhe time being, and then be chased by Night Crawler as well. And two... Let Night Crawler end your pain, let him destroy you, and end this trouble you are in.

Dream - Angel, I don't know who is chasing you, I don't know why you are running around. but one thing I do know. You and the rest of these idiots in the crowd. [The crowd boos] Will see the Nine Hells being done tomorrow night. And Angel... you can bet your bottom dollar that you'll be on the receiving end of that one!

Night Crawler’s theme begins to play. The crowd quickly begins to boo again.
Night Crawler and Dream make their way out of the ring as the show cuts to a promo:


The image fades away
