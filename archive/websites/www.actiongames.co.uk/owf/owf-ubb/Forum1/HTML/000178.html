OKORU

*The scene opens up just on a shot of a TV screen in front of a local electronics store. The show on the screen is a local newscast update on a madman loose in the city.*

News Anchor: This just in from the WLCL Roving Reporter, yet another alomst fatal injury involving an unknown assailant. We go live now to Cassandra Dee who's at the site right now. Cassandra, can you hear me?

*The scene shifts to a shot of the latest attack. It's near a liquor store and the windows in the place are shattered all over the ground. Broken liquor bottles litter the ground and a couple hundred dollars worth of wine is in the gutter pouring into the storm drain.*

Cassandra: Yes, I can hear you. This is the place where the latest attack has been. Sources say that sometime around 9 o'clock this morning, they saw a man dressed in some sort of old-time warrior outfit was seen near this area. He was covered in what people call squigly lines. The latest victim has told WLCL that the squiggly lines where Japanese characters. He says that he was just walking to the liquor store to pick up some wine for a party when he heard a loud yell. All of a sudden he was overcome by the attacker and beaten savagley. As you can see, the glass in the liquor store has been broken out due to the victim being thrown head first into the store. The only thing that we have to go on at this point are that the attacker is Oriental, he has Japanese character tattooes, and he's wearing an old-time, presumably Japanese, warrior outfit. We'll have more on these shocking events when news breaks. I'm Cassandra Dee for WLCL, the Roving Reporter. Back to you at the studio.

*The shot comes back onto the news anchor in the studio.>

News Anchor: Thank you Cassandra. Our thoughts and prayers go out to the young man who was attacked. If you or anyone you know has any information on these serious of attacks, please call the WLCL studios at...

*A loud scream is heard and a brick is thrown into the glass, breaking the TV screen. The stores alarm goes off and the person who threw the brick steps in front of the camera. It's Tokyo!(ooc: duh) He looks around and shows he's wearing a new outfit that completely covers his tattooes. He grins evily into the camera and jerks his head to the sound of sirens. He runs off and you can hear his footsteps trail off. The scene fades to black.*
