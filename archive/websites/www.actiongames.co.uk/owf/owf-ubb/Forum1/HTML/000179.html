Night Crawler
Member 		posted October 18, 2000 05:01 PM               

A scene opens somewhere downtown. Night Crawler is walking the streets with Dream. The night is dark and a light drisle can be seen on the samera's lens.

Dream - You totally demolished him, didn't you?!

Night Crawler - It had.... to be..... done....

They continue walking down the streets with the cameraman somewhere ahead of them following their conversation.

Dream - Well, Johnny No Where, is history now... we must focus on bigger and better things. One must not forget, Angel!

Night Crawler - He will be...... destroyed......

Dream - You bet your bottom dollar he will! But I am still trying to figure out, what the hell is wrong with him?

They both stop, Dream begins to talk into the camera.

Dream - What the hell is going with you Angel? Last time we saw you cutting up a promo, someone, was cutting you up... But then last night, you were healthy and ready for your tag match. Now Angel, we all know that you are weird and crazy, after all you are playing with fire when you messed up with Night Crawler.

Dream steps up in front of Night Crawler, while he stares at a near by building.


Dream - No Angel, the game stops now! Night crawler has been keeping out of your way for too long now, and frankly, we are both running out patients. Angel, Saturday Night, you and Night Crawler, will hook up! Angel, I am not asking you... I am telling you!
These games have gone long enough, if you want to see how serious we are, then just show up Saturday night, and Night Crawler will destroy you!

Night Crawler - Through..... the Nine........ Hells.........

The image fades away
