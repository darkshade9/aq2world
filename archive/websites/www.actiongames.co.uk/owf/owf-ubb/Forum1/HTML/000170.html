THE IMPECABLE

SCOTTY HINTON

*The scene opens upon a shot of the house of Canada's favorite son. It's a quaint little abode in suburban Calgary. Nice red brick house with a red and white picket fence. The front door opens and Scotty Hinton comes out wearing a nice cardigan sweater complete with loafers and jeans with a pipe hanging from his mouth. He gives his best Hugh Heffner-esque grin and speaks to his fans.*

Hinton: Hello, kiddies. Just checking the mail and all. Not much to do today. Just wait around for my next signing show.

*Hinton walks to his mail box and rifles through the envelopes found therein.*

Hinton: Bills...bills...I may be a $1 million dollar winner...more bills...ah ha!! My paycheck from the OWF. God bless Mike for paying me in Canadian dollars.

*Hinton hurridly opens the letter to see how much of the green stuff he hauled. He looks at his paycheck at can't help but grin.*

Hinton: I could show you this but I wouldn't want to embarass the other wrestlers. Speaking of embarassments, Gag, you Old Hickory you, you did it to yourself again.

*Hinton puffs his pipe and speaks again.*

Hinton: Ya see, Gag, I didn't mean to hit you with that chair when you were fighting Tokyo, who by the way is feeling 100% better. No no, I didn't mean to hit you at all. I merely ran down to the ring with a chair to sit down in the ring and get a closer view of the action. Have you ever seen the moniters this fed has? About as big as a donut hole, eh? Anyways, I simply ran into you when I ran into the ring to get a closer look. Shame shame for telling me what I did or did not do. For shame.

*Hinton walks up to his front porch and pockets his check. He sits down on his front porch swing to refill his pipe and continues.*

Hinton: And as for the fast count, I was wide awake when that beer can from that idiot fan hit me. Not only that, my friend, but I have the support to my theory from fellow Great White Northerner and OWF Commentator Johhny-All-Good. This man is a class-act. God bless 'em for sticking up for us Canadians in a country that oppresses them. Bartlett thinks he can shut him up, but he can't. So it seems now that there's two people who saw the fast count, Gag.

*Hinton finishes stuffing his pipe and puffs a little to get a fire going.*

Hinton: As for this Saturday and your tag match challenge, I'm all for it. I'll be happy to beat you and this Trey "Dim" Stevenson. That's right, Gag. Trey "Dim" Stevenson. Yes, he's a dim one if he thinks he can beat the Impecable One. But the thing is, I need a tag partner. Seeing as Tokyo's injured and probably won't be back until next week when the doc gives him clearance, I need a partner. So if anyone wants to be in the limelight and tag with the Impecable one, you just say the word and it's a date, baby. Gag and Dim, I'll you your asses in the ring.

*Hinton grins into the camera and walks back into his house. The scene fades to black.*
