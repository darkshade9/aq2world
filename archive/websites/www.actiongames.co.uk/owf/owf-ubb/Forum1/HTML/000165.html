THE IMPECABLE

SCOTTY HINTON

*The scene opens up on a frozen pond in the early morning hours in Calgary. Some steam is rising off the pond almost giving it a creepy look. Little Canadian children are getting on the school bus to go get their education and Scotty Hinton walks up to the camera with his oh-so-infamous grin.*

Hinton: Ah...Canadian mornings always make me feel all giddy and such. It just makes you proud to be apart of the greatest country on this Earth. Nuts to America, man. God blessed Canada!!

*Hinton rubs his hands together for some warmth and rubs them on his shoulders*

Hinton: Well, first up on my little agenda is Gag. Gaggy, you really sunk to a new low. Enlisting the audience to throw their beverages at me while I was set to beat your ass un, deux, trois. Shame on you, mister. Well, I can see why you needed the audience to help you cheat. You see, you know as well as I do that you were just flat out-wrestled that night. I, the Impecable One, just hands-down put a hurting on your American ass. And you just couldn't stand the thought of losing to the better man. You just had to cheat your way into a win. Your victory was indeed tainted, Gag. Not only did you incite the fans into throwing stuff, you had the ref have that fast count. Do we have the clip available? We do? Ok, run it.

*The camera then shows some footage from the Hinton/Crag match. It shows Crag doing a leap of faith to the ringside from the top rope and lands a diving elbow smash right on Hinton. He picks Hinton up and throws him into the ring then quickly goes for the pin: 1....2....3. The scene then comes back upon Hinton.*

Hinton: Now that was a fast count. So Gaggle, in conclusion, go ahead and go around telling everyone that you meet that you upheld the honor of your country by beating a smart-ass Canadian. But keep in the back of your mind that if it weren't for the beers and the fast count...I'd be the hero.

*Hinton grins again and takes a deep breath and holds his nose.*

Hinton: And to you, Stinky Russian. I wipe my hands clean of you, pal. You got some serious issues with gay sex seeing as you mention it in every idiotic, dull, and boring speech you give. Go back home to Russia, talk to a doctor, or something...'cause this whole gay thing is getting out of control. I mean, I beat you. Cleanly, I might add. Not tainted like, oh say, Crag's victory over mine. After that, you still feel the need to wrestle me. Hey Scummy, news flash...I...am...better than you. You just hide out here in Mid-CarderLand while I go on to bigger and better things.

*The scene fades to black as Hinton grins into the camera.*
