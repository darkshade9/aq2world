(The camrea fades in and Dread X is stood outside his house. Dread X is holding a sports bag over his shoulder. He looks at his watch and then starts tapping his feet impatiently)

Dread X: God damn where the hell is that limo?

(Dread X starts pacing up and down his porch. Just then a black limo pulls up to the sidewalk)

Dread X: Finally! Come on I have something to say.

(Dread X signals for the camrea man to follow him. They both get in the limo and it speeds away)

Dread X: Where the hell were you? I've been waiting 10 minutes!

Driver: Im sorry sir. Traffic is very bad nowadays.

Dread X: Yea yea now just get me to the arena!

Driver: Yes sir!

(Dread X loks at the camrea)

Dread X: Well on TNT its my 3rd match in the OWF. I have won 2 matches and now I have a .com title shot. Thats just 1 title away from the world title so I must be doing something right! Lets see, im facing Lowell DotCom. Well you have a talent you can fall back on when you leave OWF. If you get injured you can always get involed in computers! Well you might have to refresh your skills because on TNT im gonna be as good as ever because theres gold on the line. Let me tell you know im not gonna pull any punchs when im in the ring. Its me, the X man going 150% for the .com title. Get prepared Lowell, im warning you now!

Driver: Excuse me sir but get ready because we are almost there!

Dread X: Enough of Lowell for now. Its time we entered the arena.

(The limo slows down and then pulls up to the sidewalk. Dread X steps out and so does the camrea man. Dread X walks over and opens the door and goes inside the arena. The camrea man runs in front of Dread X so he can finish the promo)

Dread X: Hey how aout that Crossbones? Anyone seen him around? I hear people are worried that he might not turn up. I guess if he doesn't it will just be me and Lowell. It does'nt really matter to me. I won a triple threat last week on TNT and I beat Crag on SNS so im really looking forward to this match. It should be pretty good caus Lowell looks to be pretty good. I wish him the best for our match because he wont last.

(Dread X reaches the back where all the locker rooms are. He walks over to Blades office. He sees the list. He grabs the pen but then hesitates. He shrugs his shoulders and walks off)

Dread X: Nah maybe not. I dont wanna get involved.

(Dread X walks straight past Mikes office, but then he walks back and looks at the list. He looks for about a minute. Then he walks off again)

Dread X: I'll let someone else get involved in their fight. Its not my problem. Oh well I better get ready for my match on TNT.

(Dread X opens his locker room door and throws in his bag. He walks in and shuts the door. The camrea then looks at both lists on Mikes and Blades door then the scene fades to black)
