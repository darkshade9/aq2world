(He is Valek. A mercenary for hire. As of a few weeks ago, he was jobless, until he met the Freedom Fighters. They are a group of anarchists that plan on overunning the government. They fight for a semi-noble cause, and do all that they can to destroy the Sprinter 2000. A computer issued out to the people by who else? The government.)

*** Using Close Combat Master Skill***

Valek: What the hell do you want?
Gang leader: Just your threads, punk.
Valek: Hehe... That's it? Well, sorry to say it, but... Your going to have to kill me first!
Gang member: That can be arranged.

(At that, the seven gang members rush him, and at the same time attempt an all out asault. The first two reach him, and suffer broken noses and fractured skulls. The next three are sent to the ground... Hard. The rest of the men flee in terror, humiliating their leader.)

Gang leader: What the hell!? What are you?
Valek: Why don't you come here and find out?

(The gang leader reaches into his jacket, and begins to pull out a .45. Valek rushes him, kicks him in the chest, which sends the man flying, and then proceeds to pumble him into the floor of the dark alleyway. After several moments, Valek stands up, ajusts his trenchcoat, and grabs two of the gang members by the arm. He drags them out of the alley, onto the busy sidewalks, while at the same time conceiling his face. He drops them in the center of the walkway, and runs back down the alley, looking back over his shoulder only to make sure that no one was following him. He then slows down at the other end, and heads back to the Freedom Fighters' hideout.)
