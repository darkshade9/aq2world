Setting: Los Angeles.

As Matt approached an old abandoned warehouse, he took a deep breath and opened the door. The first thing he saw was a manhole. He took off the lid, and went down the ladder, then put the lid back on behind him. He breathed a sigh of relief when he saw that it was indeed the L.A. Freedom Fighter Headquarters. It was a simple room, with three NON-Sprinter computers, almost as rare as the Freedom Fighters themselves. He quickly sat down to rest, but didn't expect this to happen: The FBI had pin pointed the position of the headquarters and sent a police force in after Matt!

Policeman: Don't bother resisting, we've got the place surrounded!

Matt: Does it take an entire force of you "big strong men" to take out one little 14 year old?

Policeman: Don't bother with that, we know your lethal tendencies!

Matt: Oh, come on, those were 12 year olds and a robot! You're three times my size! You and me, buddy, one on one!

(Whoever controls NPC's, please finish this one up for me...)
