Steam - Cold and Wet?  by GBee at 23:57:07

Valve's Steam project is causing quite a heated debate in our forums (pun intended). Just a short time after it's official release and with their WON ID servers due to be shutdown soon there are some real concerns being expressed on a number of issues. For example only days after the release of Counter Strike 1.6 through the Steam network there are some obvious flaws and cheats. Non-broadband users are dismayed to discover that they cannot play their beloved game and some are just angry at the downtime suffered by the service. Is this the end of Half Life and it's various mods? What about Half Life 2, have Valve already damaged it's chances and will Doom 3 sneak back to the forefront to become the game of the year?
