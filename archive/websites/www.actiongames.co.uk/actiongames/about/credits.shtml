Old Staff

 RobbieBoy 

I really don't know where to begin praising Robb, he was the original founder and owner of actiongames. I met him through the TNN website which as an ActionQuake player was my bible. When the TNN website needed a new home he purchased the Action Games domain and created the Action Games collection of sites. Unfortunately, for a few months in early 2000, he was forced offline due to 'real life' issues which affected the forward growth of Action Games, but it never died. Recently Robb decided to move on from Action Games so that he could give more time to his new projects. As his successor his original ideas for the TNN site are what I hope to build upon in the future for Action Games.

I still consult him for ideas and opinions and to some extent he still works on the LTK Depot at TNN. I see Robb as a good friend and wish him all the luck for the future.
