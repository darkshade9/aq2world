Hosted Forum Customisation

You have the ability to request some customisation of your hosted forum, such as colours and some of the graphics used on the page. We use the Ultimate Bulletin Board software and are restricted to the components that it allows to be customised.

However, within its restrictions you can make significant changes to the look of your forum. This can allow you to get a reasonable match for your website colour scheme if you are linking directly.

Below is a form you can fill in to request changes to be made to your hosted forum. Colours are specified by either selecting a predefined colour from the selection box, or by entering numeric RGB value in the format #RRGGBB in the second box.

For graphics that can be changed, suggested sizes are given as a guide only. You may use different sizes, but they could look odd if too much larger or smaller than that suggested. Ideally the images should be put somewhere on a web or FTP site so that you can specify the URL of the file. If that is not possible, they may be emailed to Rincewind as attachments. However please fill in the Anything else box to say what you will be sending, or it will be ignored.

If you do not wish to change a colour or graphic simply leave the field as is.
