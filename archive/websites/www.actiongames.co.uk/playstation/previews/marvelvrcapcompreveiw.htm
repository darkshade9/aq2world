Marvel Vr. Capcom

I just received a japanese import of this game, and I have been playing it alot lately.

We all know that Street Fighter Alpha 3 is great. Accually, let me correct that statement, we all who have played it know that Street Fighter Alpha 3 is great. Some of us are bastards that won't give 2D games a try. Well I got news for you idiots, this game is fun, and it is 2D.

Now let me say some bonuses of 2D graphics, they allow for more moves, more detailed backgrounds, great looking characters, cooler moves that involve changing backgrounds and tons more. This game is great.

The game let's you start out with about 15 characters, they are:

  • Ryu

  • Chung Li
  • Captin America
  • Spidey
  • Mega-Man
  • Gambit
  • Hulk
  • Venom
  • Zangiff
  • and more that are there from the start and that you can gain.

There is a tag team feature that isn't exactly easy or full tag team, but it is there. You can choose the regular characters to be your partner, or you can choose special characters, an additional 25 or so that you can choose from.

There is Vr. Mode, Battle mode, and other modes, but trust me, the game is fun, and we should look for it in the future.
