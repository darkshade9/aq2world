MOD-CHIPS FAQ

This is the Mod-Chip faq, I know alot of you have questions about Mod-Chips, and I have answers, so e-mail me with them and I will add the answers to this section!

I know your problem, you ordered a japanese import but it doesn't work. Why? because you don't have a mod-chip. There are two kinds of mod-chips, the internal and external. I recommend the external, what you need to do is plug it into the gameshark port, don't worry, as the external is also a gameshark. A bad thing of this is that you need to do a modified swap method. You need to put a spring (that comes with the modchip) into the playsation and then put a black disc in, then when the black disc stops spinning you replace the black disc with the backup game or japanese game (and yes, copied games will work too)

Is it illegal if I copy my games?
If you make one copy of a black disc you bought it is perfectly legal, but if you make several copies and sell those copies you are in trouble.

What if I buy a copied game?
Shame Shame Shame on you and the person that sold it to you.

How can I copy my games?
There is a program called Nero 4.0 that can copy your playstation games. Just look and you will find it.

How do I install my internal Modchip?

You will have to check the instructions or the webpage from where you bought it, also, if you bought the modchip, odds are that the person that sold it to you will be happy to help you out with installing it.

Is it illegal to install a Modchip on my Playstation?

It is not, you bought the machine, it is yours, you can do what you want with it, including throwing it off a 4 story building (though that isn't reccomended) but opening your playstation will make your warrenty for your playstation invalid. This is why I reccomend external, because you don't have to open your playstation.
