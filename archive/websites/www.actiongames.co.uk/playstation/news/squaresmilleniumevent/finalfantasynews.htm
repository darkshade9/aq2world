Square's Millenium Event: Final Fantasy News

Square made alot of shocking statements about what they plan to do with the series--and beleive me, it is anything but slowing down, infact, they announced not one, not two but three friggin games, count em, three of them. I would have screenshots for ya, but square only allowed the Logos to be published, sorry ya'll (pardon my texan accent, but the fact is, I am from Wisconsin, so I guess there is not excuse for my accent....damn.)

Final Fantasy IX

Not much was said about this game, but it is said to be released this summer (the summer of 2000) and will be for the Playstation, not the playstation 2. Another thing to note about this game is it is departing from the futuristic elements in recent Final Fantasy games and is going back to the old sword and magic medievil legacy. Yoshitaka Amano is back and creating the character designs, and trust me, they will look good. Another thing is that this game is throwing away the previous pre rendered backgrounds and making polygonal backgrounds, I think this could turn out for the better, but we'll have to wait and see. As for the spells, they look good, but just like Final Fantasy 8, but how can we complain, Final Fantasy 8 was a great looking game!

Final Fantasy X

This is the first Final Fantasy that will be for the Playstation 2 and will be released in Spring of 2001. Another new thing about this is that it will take advantage of the Playstation 2 modem by being compatable with square's Play-Online strategy (see squares online strategy section) Basically what this does is it helps you out, by checking what you have missed and such. You think that is cool, wait until you read this:

Final Fantasy XI

If you thought the modem compatability of Final Fantasy X was cool, listen to this: the whole game is online! Yes, we all knew that it was inevitable, finally a Final Fantasy game goes multiplayer. Taking the Ultima Online approach, this game let's you play with people, it is the closest thing to real life because all the other characters are real people, you get to talk with them, form enemies and make friends, this will truly be a breakthrough for the Playstion 2.
