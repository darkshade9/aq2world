Square's Millenium Event: Online Strategy

Square has teamed up with NTT Communications Corporation to create it's new online strategy titled: PlayOnline. This is a huge step forward for Square and they know it. They want to get this up and ready for 2001 and they have tons of things they need to do before them, here are some:

Music:

Square plans to have top 20 charts, or some thing of the sort updated regularaly and be able to download music to listen to.

Manga:

Manga is a japanese comic type thing. I don't really care to much about this so sorry guys, go somewhere else if you want more info, but to sum it up into anything remotely interesting is that you get to look at and read Japanese comics online.

E-mail and Chat:

You get to chat with people and e-mail. Nuff said. Well I guess I can tell you some more details, basically that you get to decide who knows your real name and who doesn't, and it let's you create an online identity for yourself.

Sports:

An interesting and good idea. This not only gives you recent sports news but it also let's you download the recent stats and upload them to your games, making your games always recent. Also, you can get compete against old stats from the internet, it is sort of hard to explain, but any questions just send em in.

You get to Browse the net with a standard net browser, I can't really expand on that one.

And the rest...

You get to buy stuff online, play games online, and we all know that square has some more things up it's sleeves.
