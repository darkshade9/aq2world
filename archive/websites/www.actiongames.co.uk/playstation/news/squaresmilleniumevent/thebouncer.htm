UPDATED BOUNCER PREVIEW

Introduction:

If there is one game that I bet my life on that would be the best PS2 game so far it would have to be Square's brilliant game: The Bouncer. I mean think about it, you got Tekken Tag Tournement, surely a great game, but taken right from the arcades, Gran Turismo 2000, an updated version of a game that we currently have in our hands. Bouncer, a new game that combines beutiful graphics with an amazing gameplay system. Square has decided to take the harder route than just making a pretty game that people will buy, square has decided to make a great game.

What you basically do

Details about The Bouncer are yet to be released, but we do know that the game will span huge environments that let you interact with basically everything in sight. If you have ever seen the movie The Matrix (and if you haven't seen it I recommend that you do see it) You sort of know what this game will be like, you get to fight like that basically, if you want ot know what I am talking about, check out the movie below (found at IGN thanx IGN)
