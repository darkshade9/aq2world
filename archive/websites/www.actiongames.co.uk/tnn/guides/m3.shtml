   M3 Shotgun
 

Heckler And Koch Benelli M3 Super 90 Assault Shotgun

Statistics
Caliber 12 gauge
Magazine Capacity 7 Shells
Sight Radius 298.5 mm
Mass (Empty) 3.45 kg
Barrel Length 501.65 mm
Overall Length 1,041.4 mm
Width 57.15 mm
Height 184.15 mm

Details

This is my personal favourite AQ2 weapon, although the rate of fire is not very fast and it's effectiveness over long distances leaves quite a bit to be desired, at medium to close range this weapon is deadly (a close range hit will usually kill someone outright). The firing system is pump action and fires a single round at a time, when the time comes to reload the shells are inserted individually into the gun so it's best to go hide while you do it. There is no second attack mode for this weapon, just point and shoot.
Best uses for the M3 are in closed areas or when you're running towards the enemy like a mad man trying to draw attention away from your team's snipers.
