   MP5
 

Heckler And Koch MP5/10 Sub-Machinegun

Statistics
Caliber 10 mm Auto
Ammunition Feed Straight, synthetic magazine; 30-round capacity
Rifling 6 Groove right hand twist
Sights Rotary aperture rear sight
Sight Radius 340 mm
Mass (Empty) 2.67 kg
Barrel Length 224.8 mm
Overall Length 680 mm
Width 50 mm
Height 210 mm

Details

One of the most famous sub-machineguns ever made, the MP5 is deadly accurate and terrifyingly powerful. The MP5/10 was designed to be a compact, accurate, and reliable weapon that has almost twice as much muzzle power as the standard 9 mm MP5. In AQ2 the MP5 is the most popular weapon because the rapid rate of fire allows the user to shower an area in lead severely maiming multiple targets. The more experienced player will use the MP5's accuracy to their advantage by firing from a long range and cutting down people who have less accurate weapons such as shotguns and therefore cannot fire back very effectively. The second fire mode is a 3-round burst where you must release then re-press the trigger again after three rounds have been fired - this is useful for conserving ammo. In 3-round burst mode the gun is even more accurate and can potentially be used for sniping if you're good enough.
If you see a group of MP5s headed your way, you'd do well to find a kevlar vest quickly.
