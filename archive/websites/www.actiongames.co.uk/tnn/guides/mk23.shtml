   Mk 23 Pistol
 

Heckler And Koch Mark 23 0.45 Caliber SOCOM Pistol

Statistics
Caliber 0.45 ACP
Ammunition Feed Staggered Magazines; 12 round capacity
Rifling Polygonal Bore, Right Hand Twist
Sight Radius 197 mm
Mass (Empty) 1.21 kg
Mass (Fully Loaded) 1.576 kg
Barrel Length 149 mm
Overall Length 245 mm
Width 38.8 mm
Height 150 mm

Details

The HK Mk.23 is the pistol favoured by the US. Special Operations Command, hence it's nickname: The SOCOM. The pistol is your basic starting weapon in AQ2 but that doesn't mean it's a last resort. The 0.45 caliber pistol has enough punch to kill somebody outright from a headshot almost every time, in automatic mode it has a decent rate of fire but loses accuracy at long distances, in semi-automatic mode the rate of fire is slowed and you cannot fire continuously just by holding down the trigger but it is deadly accurate. The pistol can be fitter with a silencer or a laser sight and if you pick up an enemies pistol you can use both at once (they will be forced into automatic mode and the silencer and lasersight will not be usable) dealing out a quick death to any enemy who happens to stray too close.

Accuracy, reliability and power make this the most versatile weapon in the game, it can be used for melee firefights or even for sniping if you've got good eyesight (or IR Vision). When you're on the battelfield whatever you do, don't underestimate your standard pistol, it can save your life or in the enemy's hands, it can take it.
