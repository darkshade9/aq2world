   SSG 3000
 

SIG Sauer SSG 3000 Sniper Rifle

Statistics
Caliber 7.62 x 51 mm NATO
Magazine Capacity 6 Rounds
Scope Hensoldt 1.5-6x 42BL
Mass (Empty) 6.2 kg
Barrel Length 600 mm
Overall Length 1,180 mm

Details

Made by the Swiss company SIG, this is one of the most accurate weapons in the world and is the most accurate weapon in AQ2. The sniper rifle is not a standard run around firing madly weapon, it is a precision tool to be used by a specialist, a sniper must be patient and calm at all times but must also be alert to the situation. The SSG 3000 cannot be fitted with a lasersight, it doesn't need one - the weapon is fitted with an optical zoom scope made specifically for it which allows zoom levels of 2x, 4x, and 6x. The rounds are armour piercing so a kevlar vest doesn't help the user much, of course, if you're good enough a kevlar vest won't be an issue (you can't wear it on your head).
Some people use the rifle while running around and zoomed in at 2x or 4x, this does give more accuracy but isn't really what the weapon was designed for and the slow rate of fire means that if you do miss you're in big trouble.
When sniping the best choices of item would be a bandolier with IR vision enabled to help you find targets or a silencer so that nobody know where you're shooting from.
