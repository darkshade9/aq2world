   M26 Grenade
 

M26 Fragmentation Grenade

Statistics
Body Material Cast Iron
Mass 0.7371 kg
Filler Flaked TNT
Fuse M204A1

Details

The M26 is a very powerful weapon that can be thrown at short, medium, or long range (selectable by weapon modes), you can also 'fine tune' the distance by altering how long you hold the fire button for (hold it too long and it'll explode in your hand). The grenade has a tremendous range and if an enemy isn't killed by it they may be sent through the air for a small distance. To use a grenade in teamplay you must select the bandolier as your item and the server must have grenades enabled. If you see a grenade heading your way it's best to simply run as quickly as you can away from it, if for some reason you can't get far enough away you can crouch to reduce damage (hitting the deck).
