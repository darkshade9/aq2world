Class Guide.

This guide will take you through the 4 different styles of class available in Day of Defeat, the classes are:

Light infantry, Assault infantry, Sniper and Heavy infantry.

Light Infantry:

The general rule for light infantry is that they are the fastest class to choose although what they have in speed they lack in strengh although this is slightly balanced by being able to carry more clips and grenades than all the other classes. Both the allied and axis light infantry class carries 2 grenades and carries 10 rifle clips aswell as a pistol.

Allied weapons: M1 Garand, colt 1911 pistol, American knife and 2 fragmentation grenades.

Axis weapons: Karbiner 98k rifle, Luger '08 pistol, Hitler youth knife and 2 german stick grenades.

Assault Infantry:

This is the basic all rounder, whilst not being fastest or strongest class it certainly isnt the slowest or weakest.

Allied weapons: Thompson M1A1 sub-machine gun, colt 1911 pistol, American knife and 1 fragmentation grenade.

Axis weapons: MP40 sub-machine gun, Luger '08, Hitler youth knife and one stick grenade.

Sniper:

Probably the most favoured class in the game, the sniper is a deadly class normally killing his target in one shot although very poor at close range. On the negative side to get the best out of being a sniper you have to be extremely accurate and must be able to "set up" your rifle in a prime sniping position, most of which are in the middle of maps.

Allied weapons: springfield rifle with scope, colt 1911, American knife

Axis weapons: Gewher 43 with scope, Luger '08, hitler youth knife.

Heavy Infantry:

A very slow class but very strong and only equipted with one weapon. To make the most of a machine gunner you have to lie down (go prone) and deploy your machine gun on its tripod.(see weapon guide)
