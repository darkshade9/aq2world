AQMD Map Release Mailing List
We are now providing a mailing list that you can subsribe to, to get instant notification of when we release new maps.

This list will be sent out usually an hour or two before the site is updated - giving subscribers a slight edge on downloading the latest maps.

Links to the files will be included with the mailing so you can download directly from your email client but you will still have to visit AQMD to get more details on the maps released.

To subscribe to the mailing list, fill out the following form and click the submit button. You will then get a confirmation email and notice of any future AQ maps released by AQMD. You can also use the form to unsubscribe from the list.
