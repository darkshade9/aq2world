Thanks to all the mappers out there that have submitted their work to the AQMD, without you there would be no AQMD!

As for the ZIP structure, I mentioned it before in some news post,
but it seems like no one noticed it at all.
files should be in their own directory called "mapdocs",
and should be placed in the /action directory.

So the structure may look something like this:

\maps\*.bsp
\mapdocs\*.txt
\textures\mymap\*.wal
\env\*.tga
\sound\mymap\*.wav

etc.

That's all for now, I suppose.
