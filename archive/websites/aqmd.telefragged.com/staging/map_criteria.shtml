Map Test Criteria
#1 - Layout
  • Accessability to levels
  • Lines of fire (Sniping vs Knives/HC)
  • Balanced gameplay (No deadzones)
  • Weapons and ammo placement

#2 - Textures & sky

  • Alignment (Seams?)
  • Fits theme of map?
  • Variety (too much or none)
  • Placement (Crookedness/inappropriateness)

#3 - Prefabs

  • Placement (Logical? Does it hinder gameplay?)
  • How Used (Utility or eyecandy?)
  • Originality?
  • Fits theme?

#4 - Sounds

  • How Used (Utility or earcandy?)
  • Originality?
  • Fits theme?
  • Ambience
  • Enhances Gameplay? Or distracts?

#5 - Lighting

  • Used effectively
  • Fits theme?
  • Ambience
  • Enhances Gameplay? Or distracts?

#6 - Playability

  • High r_speeds
  • Lag?
  • Server stability
  • Server compatibility
  • Correct filenaming (all lowercase)
  • ZIP structure

#7 - Teamplay score

#8 - DM score

#9 - Originality

#10 - General Feel (Gameplay, Action?)
