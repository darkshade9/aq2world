Map Test Criteria

#1 - Layout

- Accessability to levels
- Lines of fire (Sniping vs Knives/HC)
- Balanced gameplay (No deadzones)
- Weapons and ammo placement

#2 - Textures & sky

- Alignment (Seams?)
- Fits theme of map?
- Variety (too much or none)
- Placement (Crookedness/inappropriateness)

#3 - Prefabs

- Placement (Logical? Does it hinder gameplay?)
- How Used (Utility or eyecandy?)
- Originality?
- Fits theme?

#4 - Sounds

- How Used (Utility or earcandy?)
- Originality?
- Fits theme?
- Ambience
- Enhances Gameplay? Or distracts?

#5 - Lighting

- Used effectively
- Fits theme?
- Ambience
- Enhances Gameplay? Or distracts?

#6 - Playability

- High r_speeds
- Lag?
- Server stability
- Server compatibility
- Correct filenaming (all lowercase)
- ZIP structure

#7 - Teamplay score

#8 - DM score

#9 - Originality

#10 - General Feel (Gameplay, Action?)

The total score for each map is accomplished by adding the scores for each of the areas eval'ed and then dividing by the quantity of areas that received scores. The resulting score will be between 0 and 10. Those maps that score 5 and above will be moved out of the Staging Area and posted to the AQMD Main Page. Maps scoring 5.0 and below will be returned to the mapper for reworking. If they don't wanna rework it, then it will be made available on a new area called the WORKSHOP for downloading by the public.

AQMD's Generalized Rating Descriptions

10 - This map is God's gift to Action Aquake. Perfection now has a form and this map is it! (Generally an unatainable score since nothing is perfect)
9 - This map kicks a lot of ass. You almost feel as if you are actually there.
8 - Great map. One that is gonna be an instant classic.
7 - Really good map. Fun to play on, but something is just holding it back from attaining greatness.
6 - Just slightly above average, with some work it could be great
5 - Average. Nothing special or new here just another run of the mill map.
4 - Something needs working on. People coould probably play on this map, it is just not gonna be a lot of fun.
3 - Many things need work. People trying to play on this map are likely to be frustrated.
2 - Well, a mapper would have to work really hard to score this low.
1 - The map licks ass. We can't imagine a map this bad.
