Review:
 This map is based on a police station, which some elements of the map show this. The textures on the map are a combination of realistic and cartoony. The grates, boombox, doors, book shelves, and doggy wheez treat dispenser are cartoony to say the least. Some maps pull of realistic and cartoony combo's pretty well, this however is not one of those maps. Aside from textures not mixing well, but well enough to be decent, the architecture is well done. There are some nice places to get into fights for some cool demo's or whatnot have you. One is by the flipped table that stands as a barrier on the lower of the three level building by the hall and stairs going up a floor. Another place is in the hall with the Crates stacked in the cubby hole area, on the third floor i believe it was. It's a good map overall nothing mindblowing, some things werent so enjoyable like dying from the three spikes in the doorway that kill you in 2-3 seconds if you don't move. The music from the boombox was more than aggravating though, he should stick the pimp car music from Subway in there. And one more thing which could have lead to at least another great map made in action history, the ducts above, you see them you want to crawl in them and come out and pop some people, but alas you cannot get in them. One area has one little duct area that is accessible for what could be approx. 50 Quake feet worth of ducting that was accessable. to go in crawl around a corner and fall out the bottom and into a hall which you would more than likely get massacred in if there is more than 2 people in coming through. Scores in my book rank up: Framage: 10/10 : no big areas to slow play down, not too small though. on P2 300 64meg ram no 3d card, software mode. Texture & Lighting: 7/10 : The cartoony textures didn't mix well, either all one or mixing better would have salvaged this. Weapons & ammo placement: 8/10 : Quite a bit ways of run to get to one or the other, that could be a good thing too. Some weapons appear where they should end up being a police station. Gameplay: 9/10 : I didn't play against anyone but im going to give this map the benefit of the doubt since it had some good looking fight areas Overall: 8.5/10 : Average it up and whoop. There ya go the whole shibang goes into a B map category.
-Pirate-X
