Architecture Comments:
This map is really attractive off the bat. It looks like the dam in Goldeneye. Its architecture is fantastic and has lots of treats.

Looks Rating: 8 /10
Looks Comments:
The giant dam is overwhelming. I've never seen anything quite like this in a AQ map. Textures on the damn are great. The canyon walls all over are great. Everything else looks good!!

Gameplay Rating: 9 /10
Gameplay Comments:
This is a very different Team experience. It is a BIG map. You'll probably need a fast comp to run it well (i have a PII -hehe). Team games start far off from each other and offer many ways to engage each other. There are plenty of sniping positions. Jumping off the dam is the best part! You'll hear a nice scream too! If u go down too deep in the water you'll here: "It was a bad idea comin' down this far." DM on this is half decent - hard to find weapons but very interestin!

Ambiance Rating: 0 /10
Ambiance Comments:
Feels like a warm summer day.... uhh... what am I supposed to say? It's depressing? Anyways, it makes me feel good inside... there.

Other Comments:
If u have a fast machine, play it as much as u can! If not... uh... play cracked instead.

Conclusion:
Great map BBQ! Cool sounds, original idea and great architecture! Looks great, runs great and feels great! :)

Reviewed By: *CKA* Commando

Review

Architecture Comments:
I don't think I have EVER seen a map that could ever compare to the style of this map. When I first ran the map I started at the bottom, but as soon as I looked up, I knew that this map was going to be GREAT. The effect of a tall, curved wall for the dam really made this map realsitic. I can't believe how well this map was put together.

Looks Rating: 10 /10
Looks Comments:
This map looks great! Everything, textures, water, sky, colors, are all pleasing to the eye. I love the texture he used for the dam wall, and the texture for the water. The lighting does a lot for the level, especially under water, and at the bottom of the dam.

Gameplay Rating: 9 /10
Gameplay Comments:
I recently went to a LAN party where we played Actdam for a long time. With only 5 people, this map kept us interested for a long time. When we decided to fool around on it, the map was perfect for kickboxing on the big beam across the gorge.

Ambiance Rating: 8 /10
Ambiance Comments:
All I can say is, good lighting effects, but mostly, great sound! I was real surprised when I fell off the dam and that screaming noise came. The sounds under water with the gears and generators really sounded in place.

Other Comments:
I'm not sure I like the little rooms under the towers...someone could just hide down there with no one knowing..

Conclusion:
This map is great for deathmatch mainly, I don't think it would work real good with teamplay. Other than that, this map really hit a good note with me, because it had very unusual elements that many maps dont have.

Overall Rating: 10
