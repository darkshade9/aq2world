Reviewed By: [M4A]The Rock

Review

Architecture Rating: 10/10
Architecture Comments:
Superb.

CTBCity takes the great map City, and gives it a whole new look and feel. The map gives the feel of a real city much more convincingly then anything out there at the moment. Apartment buildings are plentiful, and trashcans/cardboard boxes are scattered evenly throughout. There's even approriately placed, climbable drainpipes, giving you easy access to the rooftops.

Looks Rating: 9/10
Looks Comments:
Beautiful.

The map has a strange, purple lighting to it that is different to any other map i've seen. (Apart from the obvious original City)

Gameplay Rating: 9/10
Gameplay Comments:
The more the merrier.

This map is great for deathmatch, teamplay, and Capture the Brifcase alike. The second you get a good amount of people in the server, then things really get going. The seemingly abundant amount of windows gives great opportunities for snipers, and the narrow, twisting hallways and alleys are a hand cannon users dream. And of course there's nothing better then a bit of Karlsson magic to light up your match.

Ambiance Rating: 9/10
Ambiance Comments:
Immersive.

"fi-fi-fi-filura" by Karlsson playing on an old stereo along side a broken television in an alley-way was one of the first things i came accross when i fired up CTBCity. The ambiance is fantastic, it's realistic city setting is unmatched by any map, yes, even the popular Urban series by Gerbil! Drainpipes, alleyways, apartment buildings, trashcans and cardboard boxes are the name of the game in CTBCity.

Other Comments:
There's a new Karlsson song "fi-fi-fi-filura" for your enjoyment, but unfortunately there's always one fool who will go and kill the radio. I feel society is to blame.

Conclusion:
CTBCity will rock your world. Takes the classic City and gives it a healthy dosage of crack. Complex and immersive, CTBCity is the only map i'll be voting for on my favourite servers for a long time to come.

Reviewed By: [MM].THX

Review

Architecture Rating: 9/10
Architecture Comments:
Here again with another map review is The Rookie! Here we are looking at another excellent map from Ruskprick, specifically made for CTB.

CTBcity builds upon the original city and imrpoves it in someways. The map is a lot larger for one thing. There are more roof top areas to run around and it's basically easier to navigate around the map. Basically, pretty much better then the original City except for the flag spawn points (ugh!)

Looks Rating: 10/10
Looks Comments:
Ruskpricks bright style often reminds me of music video director MC G (Got The Life, Pretty Fly(For A White Guy).) Birght pastels are the norm in city, and while it may seem a little odd it does add to the humour factor brought on by our good friend MC Karrlson. The look of the map is just superb.

Gameplay Rating: 10/10
Gameplay Comments:
While made for CTB i can easily see TP being played on CTBcity. The level has a lot of nooks and cranies to hide in, a large area to run around in, lots of cover and cool sniper roost. It's just a perfect map.

Ambiance Rating: 10/10
Ambiance Comments:
MC Karlson, that's all I gotta say. The happy mood and look of CTBcity may seem to contrast AQ2's gritty gameplay but I actually find it hilarious in a sick sort of way. Just imagince blowing a guy away through the window of some kids window and you'll see what I mean.

Other Comments:

Conclusion:
CTBcity is another excellent map from Ruskprick. Chock full of originality, fun and overall gameplay value CTBcity should find a home on most servers.
