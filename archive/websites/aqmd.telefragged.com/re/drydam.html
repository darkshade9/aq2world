Review

Architecture Rating: 7/10
Architecture Comments:
This map almost made my servers map list. The outside area with the dry dam is really cool. The rest of it is useless. Useless for teamplay at least. Everyone hates waiting for the last players to find and kill each other. If the mine section of this map was cut out, and maybe a few more interesting structures placed outside, this map would really be fun.

Looks Rating: 7/10
Looks Comments:
Like I said before, the dry dam part looks nice. There is that block-brick building that looks way out of place. Enough so, that you notice it right away and think to yourself, "that does not look right"

Gameplay Rating: 5/10
Gameplay Comments:
The fact that it takes so long to find someone in teamplay makes this map not a good choice.

Ambiance Rating: 9/10
Ambiance Comments:
I really like the setting.

Other Comments:

Conclusion:
Too big.

Overall Rating: 6
