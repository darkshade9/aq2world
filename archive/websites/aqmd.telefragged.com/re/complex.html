Review:
Hey all!!!!! Well, since no one is reviewing this map yet, I think I'll take first crack at it. Complex is another of growing series of teamplay maps usning the "2fort" format made popular by TF. Unfourtunatley the quality of these maps range from crap (Teamassault) to Decent (Dirt) and complex in no exception.

The basic layout is a lot like the TF classic Well6, two bases facing one another with a sort of "neutral" building in the middle. The bases have two levels. The fields in front of the bases have a few climbing areas and sniper towers. It's also cute to note that there are these lasers that "zap" you if you walk into them. Very cool.

But unfourtunately the lasers are the only original thing in this level. Quite frankly when I played this map I found it to be very drab and boring. The textures are definitley uninspired and the basic outline is just a rip off of th old "2fort" style. While great for TF i find that in Action, more realistic environments are needed to help create the right atmosphere for Action Quake's squad based teamplay.

However the thing that killed this map is the gameplay aspect. Where cover is god in this game the map maker decided to keep the areas relatively open, giving you no places to bandage or reload. MP5s and M4s dominate. The strategy element is lost, turning it into a game of "shoot and deliver with an automatic."

If this map had a bit more cover, it could've been decent. But unfoutunately Complex becomes anither "2fort" map that doesn't do it for me.
- The Rookie
