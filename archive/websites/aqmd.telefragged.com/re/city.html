REVIEW
Architecture & Layout score 10/10
Commentary:
This maps layout is absoluteley fabulous! with multiple pathways to nearly everywhere, drainpipes to climb. (there are usually several on each building so it is easy to sneak up on someone if they dont watch their back) This map isn't the biggest map but it is very hard to find yur way around at first, but you get to know it. The only thing stopping me from giving this map eleven is that the dial only goes up to ten.

How It Looks score 9/10
Commentary:
Although there isn't a lot of coloured light in this map which I really love this map look excellent, but there are still some extremely well placed bits of blue which look o so sexy, with lots of new textures, the billboards on the buildings look really kickass in GL, but they are a bit blurry in software mode. The small details like street signs and police stations and a pizza resturant are extremely well done.

Gameplay (fun factor) score 10/10
Commentary:
With the excellent layout this map is fun, ive only played teamplay, but DM should be very good. This map Rocks in teamplay! as ive said with the drainpipes to climb and so on it is soooooo fun to play.

Ambiance score 8/10
Commentary:
The ambience is very good with the little flat with a stereo and tv, the stereo is playing a tune which you will actualy want to listen to, unlike the shocking one in police.

Overall score: 10/10

- Reviewed by Reedy

Review:
Rusprick did it again. After the astounding teacher i was really waiting for the next by the author, and oh my GOD. This map is already seems to be a legend amongst the community, tho some of the credit goes to Karlsson and the halirrious radio. The spectators just love it when you camp in the room and Swing It.
The textures are clear, enchanting. The map runs amazingly well, i bet thanks to the minimal use of surfaces. For example the ladders are made just as they should be, while simulating a "round" surface would make your processor steam, as seen on other maps like Urban.
The overall structure is very good, allowing the player to use many different styles and approaches. Basically the streets, with any number of roofs to deliver death from above. The balconies, stairs, rooms one can go into, and crates on the street are incredibly well placed. Not too complex, logical, and exciting. No choke points to kill teamplay, but you will not get lost either. The streets look realistic, the scale is great, and the whole thing reminds me of a Swedish suburb.
Run this map if you run a server, this is the teamplay blast of the Xmas.
- Ligur
