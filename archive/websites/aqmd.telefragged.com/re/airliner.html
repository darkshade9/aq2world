Review

Architecture Rating: 7/10
Architecture Comments:
Well, it faithfully recreates an airliner. My only problem with the layout is that it seems like you're a toddler. Last time I checked the tops of chairs generally weren't above my eye level.

Looks Rating: 3/10
Looks Comments:
This map really, really needed new textures. Seats are supposed to look plush, cockpits need a lot more than some Stroggo Q2 textures. I spawned in the cockpit and it took me a moment to remember it was supposed to be something manufactured by Boeing and not Strogg Industries Inc. I repeat - this map needs TEXTURES, and fast. It doesn't feel like an airliner at all. Windows - I thought it would be a good touch to have the windows look out onto the sky, teacher-style. That doesn't mean have an 'outdoor' area, just make the sky the window. There would have been room to insert a great cloud-top texture there. Would have made you feel like you're dozens of thousands of feet in the air instead of in a metal cocoon.

Gameplay Rating: /10
Gameplay Comments:
I cannot comment on this. Only looked around in single player.

Ambiance Rating: 5/10
Ambiance Comments:
The radio/turbulence add to the ambiance. What would have been nice for more ambiance were TEXTURES. And sky-windows!

Other Comments:
One question. The throttle between the pilots' seats - what does it do. And why can you only haul it back. :) (Maybe it makes the plane stall if you hang around long enough! Woo! That'd be neat. Whole plane pitching forward, eventually it hits the ground and everyone dies (like the apbase) and starts from scratch.)

Conclusion:
Good textures. This map needs them desperately. With them the cockpit could be made to look authentic and the seats made to look comfortable. In short, the map could be made to look like an airliner instead of just resembling one.

Overall Rating: 5
