Review

Architecture Rating: 9 /10
Architecture Comments:
This map is very original, there are lots of bumps and alot of cover. There are plenty of places to hide behind. It is large so it can be easy to get lost until you get used to the map. The large underground lake in the middle is done very well, it's sort of the center of the map, if your trying to go from one side to the other you basically have to go through it. There is also a small outside forested area which is the first well done forest i've seen in a quake2 map so far. The layout is very well done overall.

Looks Rating: 10 /10
Looks Comments:
It looks spectacular, the lighting coming through the holes in the ceiling look very real. the textures are smooth and go well with the lighting. the waterfall is nice though it could be better. the best part of this map is the forest area though. wow.

Gameplay Rating: 9 /10
Gameplay Comments:
very fun. I can see how alot of people would get bored playing this map, even 1on1 games are quite long, but people who think this map is boring just aren't intelligent players, these people have probably never heard of strategy. unlike other maps you can't just go running out into the open guns blaring and expect to get some kills like you could on teamjungle or cliff2. This takes intelligence and if your looking for a good map to sneak around on and use some hit and run tactics then this is your map. I don't play dm anymore, too easy.

Ambiance Rating: 10 /10
Ambiance Comments:
The sounds, lighting, looks and overall feeling of the map really seem to put you in the game, i've had several people say wow, you really scared me, after I'm been done killing them. You get more into the game than you do on other maps. It can either be a very dark map, if you put your gl_modulate down, although I know most of you won't. the slightest movement or sound can make you flinch and turn to see if your enemies are right behind you. It's great.

Other Comments:

Conclusion:
This map is a fun if you like slower non-rushing games and is worth downloading. I'd definatly put this map into my regular rotation if I had a dedicated server. Good job gimp, can't wait for you next map.

Review

Architecture Comments:
Definately not your typical map. No buildings whatsoever. Strictly an outdoor map with many nooms and crannies to hide in. Also some high ground to climb up on and water to hide in. The map is rather large though and will take many hours of play to learn the layout. I have to give the layout high marks just for the originality.

Looks Rating: 7 /10
Looks Comments:
Looks great ... the maps name suits it perfectly. Between swimming in the water that i assume are underground springs ... and running thru the tunnels this map is a constant surprise to the eyes. Really a surprise ... I have never seen a map even similar to this and you haven't either.

Gameplay Rating: 2 /10
Gameplay Comments:
TOO BIG!!! Unless you have a minimum of 5 man teams this map is useless in teamplay. Likewise it is to big for CTB ... I haven't played it in DM though and i think it might be ok there with a large number of players at least 8 to 10. weapons aren't always easy to find but that is just because it is so big. Although stuuning to look at and very original this map is a let down in the gameplay department. Even with a multitude of players i think the size will still be an obstacle to quick play. Be sure to set a time limit of you want to use this on a server.

Ambiance Rating: 9 /10
Ambiance Comments:
The ambiance is great. You get the feeling you are in a strange and exotic landscape and the change in the lighting from bright sunlight out in the open to dark shadow in the tunnels and the water is excellent. Runnign thruogh the tunnels not knowing if you are going to round a corner and run right into someone was a great feeling ... provided lots of excitement ... if only the map was small enough that you ran into people more often than every 2 minutes. I don't know about you but i find having to search for several minutes to findpeople to kill boring.

Other Comments:
Definately a new twist. Worth looking at i just wish it was smaller. It is a very new map and I am anxious to hear other opinios about it so download it, play it and write your own review!

Conclusion:
I would put this map on my server on a regualr basis if only it wasn't so big. I think that if it was half the size it would be perfect. I think after playing this map extensively to learn all the nooks and crannies it might be a blast to play. However taking the time to learn it just may be a bit too tedious. The originality of the map earns it an avergae score though.
