Reviewed By: AretsaM

Review

Architecture Comments:
This is a well layed out map with a good selection of areas to run about in. The house is okay (with ren + stimpy on the t.v.)but you won't be there very long. The outside area is just the right size so that you can pick off people from a distance with the humble pistol (a favoured tactic of mine) and there is a dark area on top of the lighthouse for snipers (booooo!!!). The funniest thing on this map is shooting people as they swim about in the sea trying to get to dry land, and the gardens and tunnel systems are cool as well.

Looks Rating: 7 /10
Looks Comments:
Looks arent everything, but this level runs pretty well in software mode, but running on a voodoo3 it runs like a dream. However, the textures are only okay, outside it is just sand and grass(i know its a beach so perhaps ill let the author off on that one) and i like the garden. As i said before you dont really spend much time in the house as there isnt much in there, but it looks nice. The boat is a neat touch, and lighting is used appropriately. Overall quite good.

Gameplay Rating: 10 /10
Gameplay Comments:
This is where this map shines. There are just so many ways to play it, that its hard not to love it. Running about the garden, jumping the fountaint and diving behind hedges during a shoot out is great, and there arent many places for snipers (the house balcony and the actual lighthouse are the only decent ones), but the level is designed in such a way that even snipers wont be safe if they dont move about a bit. Underwater fights are a laugh-just remember to come up for air!!! If your bored go watch ren and stimpy on the t.v. in the house-a welcome distraction from the mindless violence!

Ambiance Rating: 8 /10
Ambiance Comments:
This map isnt really where youd expect a big gunfight to take place, but its surprisingly fun. There are good well lit areas, and a few dark spots. You will spend most of your time on the beach though, as that is where most of the weapons are, and also where most of the gunfights take place. The house is really just a safe-base to bandage and pick up a few clips. The garden is good, creating a good mood when you gun someone down as they despretely try to hide behind a hedge or make it to the shed. On the beach is good as its dark, so darker skins come in ahndy here, and its nice and big, not to mention the huge sea off the pier. The map creates a wonderful mood that allows you to enjoy the map a lot, and is a welcome break from all the city levels with their drab brown textures and skyscrapers.Top stuff!

Other Comments:
I'm still undecided as to what score i should give weapon placement. All the weapons seem to be mainly situated on the beach/cliff area, and while this means you spend a lot of time there, it also means you may find yourself being picked off just as you reach a decent weapon. Perhaps more weapons in the house or garden would have been better.

Conclusion:
Overall a great map, lots to see, plenty of places to kill people in and good overall layout. As i said before, its a nice break from all the other maps that are getting very similar nowadays.
