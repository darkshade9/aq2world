Review

Architecture Rating: 9/10
Architecture Comments:
I would rate the architecture a 9 for one reason and one reason only: The train is difficult to controll. There should be more switches to start and stop the train. Other than that the architecture is perfect. The outdoor city area is a very nice touch, and the flooded bathroom is cool.

Looks Rating: 10/10
Looks Comments:
Once again T.T.I hands us a beautifully done map with all origional textures, great ambience, and the his usual attention to detail. What he did with this map, is like a combo between subway2 and urban. You have the large city area, riddled with sniper spots naturally, and then you have the underground subway area, complete with waterfalls, electrified tracks, and for once, the train actually moves! I love how this map looks hence the rating.

Gameplay Rating: 7/10
Gameplay Comments:
This I rated a little lower than the others. The reason is that picture this: you spawn on the top of the building. Your lookin around for five minutes thinkin your hot stuff. Then you realize, that you have no clue where the other guy is. The map is so huge, that you can spend the entire time just looking for somebody, much less trying to kill them.

Ambiance Rating: 9/10
Ambiance Comments:
This was also pretty good. You can deffinitely tell where you are, but you almost never know where your enemy is. This is nice because it adds an added degree of suspense, and then all of the sudden, BAM!!! you get smacked with a handcannon. This makes not only teamplay, but death match not only a great challenge, but a lot of fun

Other Comments:

Conclusion:
Overall I think this was an excellent map. T.T.I clearly has some mad mapping skills as he has demonstrated in the past. This map, next to actcity3 and urban, has got to be my favorite map of all. So why are you even bothering to read this conclusion? Go get the fricken map!!!

Overall Rating: 9

Reviewed By: =Sith=Action

Review

Architecture Rating: 8/10
Architecture Comments:
The basic layout is very well done and there are no real camping spots. My only problem was with the tall buildings, they should have some bridges or walkways connecting them to make sniper hunting a bit easier. Other than that i thought that this map was very well designed and can be enjoyed by a handcannoner or a sniper alike.

Looks Rating: 9/10
Looks Comments:
In my opinion, this is one of the nicest looking action maps. The design is simple with nice looking textures and posters on the wall. Also the little features are cool, like the flooding toilet, or the small street signs. Unlike the earlier subway maps, this map actually looks like an actual subway.

Gameplay Rating: 9/10
Gameplay Comments:
This map is extremely well balanced and is well suited for any weapon. You can ambush automonkeys at the stairs with shotguns, snipe people from the roof, spray&pray on the street, knife people on the dark corridors and the handgrenades aren't too deadly on this map.
Also the fact that games are usually short because of the good layout, makes this map very fun to play. The only major fault in this map is that most of the fighting happens on the rooftops instead of the subway.

Ambiance Rating: 10/10
Ambiance Comments:
The ambiance is absolutely perfect. It actually feels like an subway, with the flooding toilet and a small outdoor area. The corridors are suitably eerie and the ads on the walls look nice.

Other Comments:
The outside area feels rather empty. It doesn't affect the gameplay but it could have been nicer if the author added some cars or something to the streets.

Conclusion:
This map became current AQ2 favorite map after i played some games on this. It looks great, plays even better and even I can get some frags on this map.

Architecture Rating: 7/10
Architecture Comments:
Ok the layout is just wicked. The subway runs under an urban like level with a similar feel to urban2. Allowing for close in combat in the subway and sniping on the roof tops. What makes these roof tops different is you can jump from one to the other very safely; meaning that you can jump on to the roof top of a unsuspecing sniper and point blank him with a hand cannon. What would have made this map truly the best would have been if you could get inside the buildings and come up into them through the subway ect.

Looks Rating: 6/10
Looks Comments:
The textures are a little bit budget but it feels good aswell as different. The backdrop on the level is also a cool change. It is perhaps not as detailed as the other subways but this improves the lag and r_speeds.

Gameplay Rating: 7/10
Gameplay Comments:
It has a new feel to it and breaths life back into the city style map the only thing holding it back is the fact that you can't get inside the sky scrapers. The ladders are well designed so that you got fall off all the time makeing this map pure skill.

Ambiance Rating: 5/10

Other Comments:
Subway?? The game is played in the city on this map and in teamplay I found my self on the roof tops for 90% of the game. In the next subway map the players should be forced into the subway as it does make a cool battle field.

Conclusion:
The map is good fun and with an update or two (allowing players into the buildings) would make this map a real winner.
