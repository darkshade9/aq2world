LATEST NEWS

1900/6 May. 99 - Welcome to my first update for the Resources section. In case you don't know me, I'm Holy Goat, Goat for short; mapper, player etc.. I've beem with AQ2 since .91 or thereabouts; my first released map Cinema was the first non-team-made map for AQ2, Asylum is my most 'famed' work, and I'm currently working on Asylum2 for display at E3. I'm also a member of Clan FEAR, winners of the UKAQ2L, UKAQ2L cup etc., who've never been beaten in a 4v4 (21 matches and counting), which is nice. I also make skies, and play a lot.

I'll be here, looking out for Map Resources, and answering your queries about maps, editing etc.. My Telefragged mail account isn't up yet, but the link is below. If you have any skies etc., do tell me about them!

More updates this weekend probably. Have fun!

Holy Goat

1615/27 Apr. 99 - Sorry for the lack of updates this month. I've had a major family emergency that's still ongoing. I'm not even certain that I'll be able to continue maintaining this webpage through this summer, because of all the time this crisis is consuming.

In happier news, 3 great new skies from 3 of our usual sky artist contributers. Be sure to check them out!

Skull N. Bones

1430/1 Apr. 99 - Well, it's April fools day, and I haven't gotten to pull a single joke yet today. I've been too busy adding some more stuff to the skies pages. We now have all of the Westclox skies, PLUS two new ones! We also got another new sky from MJC that looks really great too! The new ones from Westclox are called, Free, and Wruins. The new MJC sky is called Stonehenge. Check all of them out.

Also, got some new textures for use in your maps. It's a collection of famous paintings converted into .wal files for use in Quake2 maps, just in case anyone wants to add another wing to the museum map.

Skull N. Bones

1300/29 Mar. 99 - We got 2 more of Westclox' sky zips in today for all of us mappers to use. They were sent in to me by Westclox himself! We should have the rest up in a couple days.
