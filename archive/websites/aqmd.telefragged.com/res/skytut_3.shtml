Sky Tutorial (Part 3 of 5)
by [PnC]Muzma
Now, the one and only sky setting

Go into the Sky&Fog section and pull down the options, choosing Edit Sky/Fog


Fig 4

In there, make sure that the option ‘Link sun to view’ is turned off /not ticked. This is very important as it makes the sun stay put when rotating the camera (which we will be doing later), otherwise, as the name suggests, the sun will re-align in relation to the camera position.

This option might be somewhere different in Bryce2, just remember to find it and turn it off or the whole effect will be lost.
