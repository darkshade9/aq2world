Well, what can be said about the Handcannon other than a huge BOOM! Because that's exactly what it sounds like when you fire from this mammoth of a power weapon. Although its dimensions are not very big (it's the 4th smallest weapon, including the grenade and knife; it's the http://www.telefragged.com/mirrors/mirror.pl?action/maps/cliff.zip">cliff , with the Handcannon, then go to the top of the cliff, and fire down at the lower station. You should see the pellets spreading out all over the back mountain. Even though the shell is filled with buckshot, which should shoot 8 pellets at a time, the Handcannon shoots about 20-30 pellets, for better game balance. So if the map is enclosed, such as a building, train, or if there are many indoor areas, the Handcannon is the one to choose.
